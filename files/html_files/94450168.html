Recordings:

Translation: Spoon
Part of Speech: noun animate
Meanings:
  • spoon
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: P'tew aq amqwanji'j jugwa'tuwi.
    Translation: Bring the broth and a spoon to me.
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: am路hkwan路chiich

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • amqwanji'jg -- spoons -- (plural)
  • amqwanji'tl -- a spoon -- (fourth person)
