maqtawamugsit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: It(shirt) is blackish
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • blackish
  • darkish
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ge' jugwa'tui maqtawamugsit ntatla'iem.
    Translation: Would you bring me my darkish colored shirt.

Pronunciation Guide: mahk·ta·wa·muk·sit
