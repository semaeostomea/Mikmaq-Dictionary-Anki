apt'sqa'muatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she locks it for him
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • lock out
  • lock in
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Apt'sqa'muatl Sapatisal.
    Translation: He/she locks the door on Jean-Batiste.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: ap·tês·hkaa·mu·a·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • apt'sqa'maq -- apt'sqa'maq -- I lock him/her out, I lock him/her in -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • apt'sqa'mul -- apt'sqa'mul -- I lock you out, I lock you in -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • apt'sqa'muit -- apt'sqa'muit -- He/she locks me out, he/she locks me in -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
