gewjipnewjit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she feels his/her hands are cold
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • feel hands cold
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Apua'tui npitnnji'tl gewjipnewji.
    Translation: Warm up my little hands their cold.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: gew·chi·bê·new·chit

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • gewjipnewji -- I feel my hands are cold -- (first person singular animate)
  • gewjipnewjieg -- We feel our hands are cold -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • gewjipnewjultieg -- We feel our hands are cold -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
