eltaqanewet

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm

Translation: He/she is spinning (yarn)
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • spin (yarn)
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Te'sigisg'g wesgowa'sit eltaqanewet.
    Translation: Everyday he/she spends his/her time spinning.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: el·ta·hga·ne·wet

Alternate Grammatical Forms:

  • eltaqaneweieg -- We are spinning -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • eltaqanewa'utieg -- We are spinning -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
