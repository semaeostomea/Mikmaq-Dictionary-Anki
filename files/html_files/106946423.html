gisigwenatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she raised him/her
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • grow
  • raise
  • adopt
  • rear
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Nsugwis pugwelniji no'guma(g) gisigwenaji.
    Translation: My aunt has raised many members of my family.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: gi·si·gwe·na·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • gisigweng -- I raised him/her -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • gisigwenul -- I raised you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • gisigwenit -- He/she raised me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
