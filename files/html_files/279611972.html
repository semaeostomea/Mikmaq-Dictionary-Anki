getmete'mat

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she finished his/hers off
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • finished off
  • wiped out
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Pie'l getmete'mateg, na apoqonmuip ni'newe'g.
    Translation: When Peter finished his off, he helped me with mine.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: ge·dê·me·dee·mat

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • getmete'mai -- I finished mine off -- (first person singular animate)
  • getmete'maieg -- we finished ours off -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • getmete'ma'tieg -- we finished ours off -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
