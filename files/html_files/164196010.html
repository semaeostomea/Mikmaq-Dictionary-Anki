pugtewu'lgw

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Steamboat
Part of Speech: noun inanimate
Meanings:
  • steamboat
  • steamship
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Pugtewu'lgw na apjiw pipugweg ta'n tujiw oqwa'q .
    Translation: A steamship always blows it's horn when it is coming in (to port).
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: buk路te路wuulkw

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • pugtewu'lgul -- steamboats -- (plural)
