wetapegsit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm

Translation: He/she descends from
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • descend from
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Tami wetapegsit Pie'l Pigie'l?
    Translation: Where does Peter Vicaire decend from?
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: we路da路bek路sit

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • wetapegsi -- I descend from -- (first person singular animate)
  • wetapegsieg -- We descend from -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • wetapegsultieg -- We descend from -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
