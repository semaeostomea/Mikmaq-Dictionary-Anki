tepilugwet

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she works enough to...
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • work enough to...
  • work enough for...
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Teplugwet gi's, enmigimanej.
    Translation: He/she has already done enough work, let's send him/her home.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: de路bi路lu路gwet

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • tepilugwei -- I work enough to... -- (first person singular animate)
  • tepilugweieg -- we work enough to... -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • tepilugutieg -- we work enough to... -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
