elteja'latl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she takes someone by vehicle toward
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • take by vehicle toward
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Utang elteja'latl ugjignaml.
    Translation: He/she is driving his/her younger brother to town.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: ell·de·jaa·la·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • elteja'l'g -- el'teja'l'k -- I take him by vehicle toward -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • elteja'lul -- el'teja'lul -- I take you by vehicle toward -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • elteja'lit -- el'teja'lit -- He/she takes me by vehicle toward -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
