nugtesguatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she crushes it with feet
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • crush (with feet)
  • soften (with feet)
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Nugtesguatl gastulastu'l gesgmnaq elsma'sigw.
    Translation: He/she softens the cedar boughs with his/her feet before laying down.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: nuk·tes·ku·a·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • nugtesgaq -- I crush it with my feet -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
