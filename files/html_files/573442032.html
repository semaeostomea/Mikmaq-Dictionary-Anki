nutapit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw

  • 1.

    Translation: He/she scouts
    Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
    Meanings:
    • scout (around)
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: So'qwa'ti'gw najinutapi'gw.
      Translation: We're going up the woods to scout around.
      Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
    Pronunciation Guide: nu·da·bit

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • nutapi -- I scout -- (first person singular animate)
    • nutapieg -- We scout -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
    • nutapultieg -- We scout -- (first person plural exclusive animate)

  • 2.

    Translation: Scout
    Part of Speech: noun animate
    Meanings:
    • scout
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Nutapit ta'nug maja'sit?
      Translation: When is the scout leaving?
      Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
    Pronunciation Guide: nu·da·bit

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • nutapijit -- scouts (two) -- (dual)
    • nutapultijig -- scouts -- (plural)
    • nutapilitl -- a scout -- (fourth person)
