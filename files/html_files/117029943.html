qasgusia'qamigt

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw

  • 1.

    Translation: Cedar grove
    Part of Speech: noun inanimate
    Meanings:
    • cedar grove
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: So'qwiei aq natgwilm mesgi'g qasgusia'qamigt.
      Translation: I'm going up the woods to look for a large cedar grove.
      Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
    Pronunciation Guide: hgas·ku·si·aa·hga·mikt

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • qasgusia'qamigtug -- at the cedar grove -- (locative)

  • 2.

    Translation: Tide Head, New Brunswick
    Part of Speech: loc
    Meanings:
    • Tide Head, New Brunswick
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Qasgusia'qamigt eltaieg sapo'nug, getu' wije'wieg?
      Translation: We are going to Tide Head tomorrow, do you want to join us?
      Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
    Pronunciation Guide: hgas·ku·si·aa·hga·mikt

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • Qasgusia'qamigtug -- At Tide Head -- (locative)
