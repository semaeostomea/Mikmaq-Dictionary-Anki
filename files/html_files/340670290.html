tpi'tnewaqan

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw

  • 1.

    Translation: Goods for sharing
    Part of Speech: noun inanimate
    Meanings:
    • goods for sharing
    • goods for distribution
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Tepgisa'tu tpi'tnewaqan, esgwiaq ginuewe'tew.
      Translation: Set aside the goods for sharing, what's left over will be ours.
      Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
    Pronunciation Guide: êt·pii·dê·ne·wa·hgan

  • 2.

    Translation: Goods for relief
    Part of Speech: noun inanimate
    Meanings:
    • goods for relief
    • relief
    • welfare
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Amsgwes pugwelg'p elita'sij tpi'tnewaqan.
      Translation: In the past many depended on relief.
      Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
    Pronunciation Guide: êt·pii·dê·ne·wa·hgan

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • tpi'tnewaqann -- goods for relief -- (plural)
