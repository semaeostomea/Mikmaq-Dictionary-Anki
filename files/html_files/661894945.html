wenaqtesguatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw

  • 1.

    Translation: He/she/it flushes out...
    Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
    Meanings:
    • flush out...
    • flush bird out of field
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Nti gisiunaqtesguatl na plawejl.
      Translation: My dog just flushed out that partridge.
      Recording: Recording by jnw
    Pronunciation Guide: we路nahk路tes路gu路a路d锚l

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • wenaqtesgaq -- I flush him/her out -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
    • wenaqtesgul -- I flush you out -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
    • wenaqtesguit -- He/she flushes me out -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)

  • 2.

    Translation: He/she kicks it upward
    Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
    Meanings:
    • kick upward
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Ge' angam ta'n telgneg wenaqtesguatl na tu'aqann.
      Translation: Look how far up he/she kicks that ball.
      Recording: Recording by dmm
    Pronunciation Guide: we路nahk路tes路gu路a路d锚l

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • wenaqtesgaq -- I kick it up -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
    • wenaqtesgaqig -- I kick them up -- (first person singular animate subject, third person plural animate object)
