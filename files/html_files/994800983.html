npisune'get

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she gathers medicines
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • gathers medicines/remedies
  • provides medicines/remedies
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Npisune'getaq, pugjig na petawletew.
    Translation: He/she's gone to gather medicine, soon he/she will be carrying it in.
    Recording: Recording by jnw

Alternate Grammatical Forms:

  • npisue'ga'tieg -- We gather medicine -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
