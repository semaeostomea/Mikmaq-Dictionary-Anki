tmaqan

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Pipe(for smoking)
Part of Speech: noun animate
Meanings:
  • pipe (for smoking)
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Metua's'g nugu' magasan ta'n tmaqang etlintuisgetasi'tij.
    Translation: These days it is difficult to find a store that sells pipes.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: dê·ma·hgan

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • tmaqang -- pipes -- (plural)
  • tmaqann -- a pipe -- (fourth person)
