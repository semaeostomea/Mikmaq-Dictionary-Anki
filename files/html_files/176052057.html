pijiet

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw

  • 1.

    Translation: He/she falls in
    Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
    Meanings:
    • fall in
    • drop in
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Sepei elmaqegigtug gisipijiet.
      Translation: He/she fell into a hole this morning.
      Recording: Recording by jnw
    Pronunciation Guide: bi路ji路et

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • pijiei -- I fall in -- (first person singular animate)
    • pijieieg -- We fall in -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
    • pijia'tieg -- We fall in -- (first person plural exclusive animate)

  • 2.

    Translation: He/she goes into debt
    Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
    Meanings:
    • go into debt
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Njignam gisipijiet, muta pilei utepaqan.
      Translation: My younger brother has gone into debt because he has a new vehicle.
      Recording: Recording by jnw
    Pronunciation Guide: bi路ji路et

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • pijiei -- I go into debt -- (first person singular animate)
    • pijieieg -- We go into debt -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
    • pijia'tieg -- We go into debt -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
