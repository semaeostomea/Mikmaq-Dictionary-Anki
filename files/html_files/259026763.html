getalqigwalatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is digging it(bug) out
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • take out by scraping
  • digging out
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Lpa getalqigwalatl ugtejgewe'l tap'tann maqamigewigtug.
    Translation: He/she dug the last potato out of the ground.
    Recording: Recording by jnw

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • getalqiwalg -- I dig it out -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
