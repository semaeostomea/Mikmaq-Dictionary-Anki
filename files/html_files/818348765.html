gisapsgnatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she gets a hold of him/her
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • able to reach
  • get a hold of
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: L'patuj gisapsgnatl ugti'l aq enma'latl.
    Translation: The boy gets a hold of his dog and takes it home.
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: gi·saps·kê·na·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • gisapsgng -- I get a hold of him/her -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • gisapsgnul -- I get a hold of you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • gisapsgnit -- He/she gets a hold of me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
