weleiwet

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she treats others well
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • treat others well
  • kind to others
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Gi'l na mawuleiwen!
    Translation: You are always very kind to others!
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: wel·ey·wet

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • weleiwei -- I treat others well -- (first person singular animate)
  • weleiweieg -- we treat others well -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • weleiwa'tieg -- we treat others well -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
Document References:
  • Pacifique Dictionary Manuscript, Page Image #234
    Transcription:2. oeleioei, être bienfaisant, bien disposé rendre bon
    Transliteration:2. weleiwei, to be beneficent, well-disposed make good
  • Dictionary of the language of the Micmac Indians : who reside in Nova Scotia, New Brunswick, Prince Edward Island, Cape Breton and Newfoundland, Page Image #46
    Transcription:Beneficent, Booskenadooōděgā’; Wělmŭdoo.
    Transliteration:Beneficent, Pusginatuo’tegei; Welm’tu.
  • Rand's Micmac dictionary from phonographic word-lists - transcribed and alphabetically arranged, with a grammar and list of place-names by Jeremiah S. Clark, Page Image #145
    Transcription:nadooádegā, I am charitable, kind-hearted, generous;
    Transliteration:natua’tegei, I am charitable, kind-hearted, generous;
