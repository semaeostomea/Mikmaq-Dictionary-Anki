nasaqomgigwatg

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she fits a handle in or on something
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • fit handle in or on (axe or hammer)
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ntlamugsis etlnasaqomgigwatg pilei nnaqanatgw.
    Translation: My uncle is fitting a new axe handle.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: na·sa·hgom·ki·gwatk

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • nasaqomgigwatm -- I fit a handle in or on something -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • nasaqomgigwatmann -- I fit handles in or on somethings -- (first person singular animate subject, third person plural animate object)
