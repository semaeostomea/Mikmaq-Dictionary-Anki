espamgo'tas'g

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: It is piled high
Part of Speech: verb inanimate intransitive
Meanings:
  • piled high
  • stacked high
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Wesamiespamgo'tas'gl na wi'gatignn nisiatal.
    Translation: Those books are piled too high they'll fall down.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: es·pam·koo·da·sêk

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • espamgo'tas'gl -- they are piled high -- (third person plural inanimate)
