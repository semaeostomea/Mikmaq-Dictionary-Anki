Mi'gmewei

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw

  • 1.

    Translation: Of the Mi'gmaq
    Part of Speech: noun inanimate
    Meanings:
    • pertaining to Mi'gmaq
    • belongs to Mi'gmaq
    • of Mi'gmaq
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Tami weja'tu's'pnn Mi'gmewe'l?
      Translation: Where did you get the Mi'gmaq ones?
      Recording: Recording by dmm
    Pronunciation Guide: mii路g锚路me路wey

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • Mi'gmewe'l -- pertaining to Mi'gmaq -- (plural)

  • 2.

    Translation: Of the Mi'gmaq
    Part of Speech: noun animate
    Meanings:
    • pertaining to Mi'gmaq
    • belongs to Mi'gmaq
    • of Mi'gmaq
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Tami weja'les'pnig Mi'gmewe'g?
      Translation: Where did you get the Mi'gmaq ones?
      Recording: Recording by dmm
    Pronunciation Guide: mii路g锚路me路wey

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • Mi'gmewe'g -- pertaining to Mi'gmaq -- (plural)
