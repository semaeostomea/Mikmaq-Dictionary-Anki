ugjigsu'g

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Family
Part of Speech: noun animate
Meanings:
  • family
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Amuj pana wen megitelmajig ugjigsu'g.
    Translation: One has to be proud of their family.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: uk·chig·suuk

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • ntjigsu'g -- my family -- (1(3))
  • njigsu'nal -- our families -- (1x(3p))
