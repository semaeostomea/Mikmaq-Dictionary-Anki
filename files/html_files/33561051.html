ewnasgwetesing

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is dizzy from a blow or fall
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • dizzy caused by a blow or fall
  • dazed caused by a blow or fall
  • confused caused by a blow or fall
  • dizzy caused by a lot of head movement
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ewnasgwetesing nitap nisieteg.
    Translation: My friend was dazed when he/she fell.
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: ew·nas·kwe·de·sink

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • ewnasgwetesin -- I am dizzy from a blow or fall -- (first person singular animate)
  • ewnasgwetesineg -- We are dizzy from a blow or fall -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • ewnasgwetesultieg -- We are dizzy from a a blow or fall -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
