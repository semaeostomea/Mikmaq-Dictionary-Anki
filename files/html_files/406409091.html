nipa'sit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she travels at night, or takes an overnight trip
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • travel at night
  • take an overnight trip
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Pusginipa'sit Muliang eliej.
    Translation: He/she usually travels at night when he/she goes to Montreal.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 沐
Pronunciation Guide: niﾂｷbaaﾂｷsit

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • nipa'tieg -- we travel at night -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • nipitaieg -- we travel at night -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
