musigta'sit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: It(tree) is cleared by cutting
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • clear by cutting
  • clear by chopping off
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ula mntn wejgupuguit gaqmusigta'sit.
    Translation: This mountain facing us is clear cut.
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: mu路sik路taa路sit
