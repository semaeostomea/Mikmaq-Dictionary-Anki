najimsnaqami'get

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is going there to repair snowshoes
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • going there to repair snowshoes
  • repair snowshoes
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Eliei Gesgapegiaq najimsnaqami'gei.
    Translation: I'm going to Gesgapegiaq to repair snowshoes.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: na路jim路s锚n路a路hga路mii路get

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • najimsnaqami'gei -- I am going there to repair snowshoes -- (first person singular animate)
  • najimsnaqami'geieg -- we are going there to repair snowshoes -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • najimsnaqami'ga'tieg -- we are going there to repair snowshoes -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
