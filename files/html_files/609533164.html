sisipewe'get

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she hunts birds.
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • Hunts birds.
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Sisipewe'ga'tieg sapo'nug.
    Translation: We are going bird hunting tomorrow.
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: si·sip·ew·ee·ket

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • Sisipewe'geieg -- We hunt birds -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • Sisipewe'ga'tieg -- We hunt birds -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
Document References:
  • Pacifique Dictionary Manuscript, Page Image #598
