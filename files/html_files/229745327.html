megnatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw

  • 1.

    Translation: He/she picks him/her up
    Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
    Meanings:
    • pick up
    • gather
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Gisigui'sgw megnatl uji'jl aq tepa'latl ugplaqaneg.
      Translation: The old lady picks up her grandchild and places him/her on her lap.
      Recording: Recording by ewm
    Pronunciation Guide: me路g锚路na路d锚l

  • 2.

    Translation: He/she chooses him/her
    Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
    Meanings:
    • choose
    • select
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Megnatl newte'tnitl napui'gignn.
      Translation: He/she chooses one photograph.
      Recording: Recording by dmm
    Pronunciation Guide: me路g锚路na路d锚l

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • megng, megn'g -- I pick him/her up -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
    • megnul -- I pick you up -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
    • megnit -- He/she picks me up -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
