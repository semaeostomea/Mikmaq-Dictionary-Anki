teppilg

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm

Translation: He/she hoists it on board
Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
Meanings:
  • hoist aboard
  • haul or rope aboard
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Gigjepugu'tg ms't goqwei ta'n telteppilg glaman ma mnja'qtnug.
    Translation: He/she pays close attention to how he/she hoists everything aboard so nothings will fall off.
