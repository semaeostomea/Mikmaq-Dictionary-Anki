elisgnualatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she knits it
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • braid
  • knit (sweater)
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Nsugwis teluet elisgnualatl gisu'e'nitl wesgitl.
    Translation: My aunt says she is going to knit a warm vest.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: e·lis·kê·nu·a·la·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • elisgnualg -- I knit it -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
