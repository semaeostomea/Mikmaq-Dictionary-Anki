milamugsit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw

  • 1.

    Translation: It(shirt) is multi-colored or many colors
    Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
    Meanings:
    • multi-colored
    • different colors
    • variety of colors
    • many colors
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Milamugsit mimajuinu ula ugs'tqamu.
      Translation: There are people of different colors in this world.
      Recording: Recording by jnw
    Pronunciation Guide: mi路la路muk路sit

  • 2.

    Translation: Many different kinds
    Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
    Meanings:
    • many different kinds
    • many different sorts
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Gaqi'similamugsit miti's ula gm'tginug.
      Translation: There are many different kinds of trees here in our territory.
      Recording: Recording by ewm
    Pronunciation Guide: mi路la路muk路sit
