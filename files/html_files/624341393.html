menqawet

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she brings in the wash (laundry)
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • bring in the wash (laundry)
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Miamuj nugu' ms't goqwei gaqipiateg, pe' menqaweiap.
    Translation: I expect everything is dry by now, I'll bring in the laundry.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: men路hga路wet

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • menqawei -- I bring in the wash (laundry) -- (first person singular animate)
  • menqaweieg -- we bring in the wash (laundry) -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • menqawa'tieg -- we bring in the wash (laundry) -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
