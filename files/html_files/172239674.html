mimugwalatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she hides him/her
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • hide
  • conceal
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Apsute'gan mimugwalg.
    Translation: I hide the doll.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: mi·mu·gwa·la·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • mimugwalg -- I hide him/her -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • mimugwalul -- I hide you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • mimugwalit -- He/she hides me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
