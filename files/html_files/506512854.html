nipiwisugwet

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: he/she cooks at night (for others)
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • cooks at night (for others)
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Pie'l nipiwisugwet muta uji'jg peita'nitjig saponug.
    Translation: Peter is cooking at night because his grand children will arrive tomorrow.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: nibi路wis路ug路wet

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • nipiwisugweieg -- we cook at night (for others) -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • nipwisugwa'tieg -- we cook at night (for others) -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
Document References:
  • Pacifique Dictionary Manuscript, Page Image #607
    Transcription:nipoisogoei, faire la cuisine d. n.
    Expanded:nipoisogoei, faire la cuisine de nuit
    Transliteration:nipwisugwei, cook at night
    Source as entry:nipwisugwei, cook at night
    Normalized source as entry:nipwisugwet, he/she cooks at night (for others)
