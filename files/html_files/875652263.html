esmatmugsit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she/it is attractive
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • attractive
  • pretty
  • lovely
  • nice
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Esmatmugsit na gnnjanji'j!
    Translation: Your little child is lovely!
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: e·sê·ma·têm·uk·sit

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • esmatmugsi -- I look attractive -- (first person singular animate)
  • esmatmugsieg -- we look attractive -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • esmatmugsultieg -- we look attractive -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
