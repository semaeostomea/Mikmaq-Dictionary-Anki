gejiatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is aware of him
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • aware
  • know about
  • have knowledge of
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Gejiatl ta'n telqamigsinij ugtue'ml.
    Translation: He/she knows his/her dog's temperment.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: ge路ji路a路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • geji'g -- I know about him -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • geju'l -- I know about you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • geji'lit -- He/she knows about me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
