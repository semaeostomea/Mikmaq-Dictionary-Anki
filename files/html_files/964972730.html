sasapegwit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Seabird with wings too short to fly
Part of Speech: noun animate
Meanings:
  • seabird with wings too short to fly
  • penguin
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Apaqtuweiaqi'g na sasapegwit'pnig aq mu gisialaqsultigupni'g.
    Translation: The Great Auks were sasapegwitaq and they were flightless.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: sa路sap路e路gwit

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • sasapegwitaq -- seabirds with wings too short to fly -- (plural)
