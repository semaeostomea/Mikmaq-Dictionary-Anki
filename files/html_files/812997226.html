piluowei

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Bullet
Part of Speech: noun inanimate
Meanings:
  • bullet
  • projectile
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Mego'tigl na nige' piluowe'l, mu st'ge' amgwes.
    Translation: Bullets are expensive now, not like at one time.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: bi路lu路o路wey

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • piluowe'l -- bullets -- (plural)
