neps'tgwa'latl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she raises him /her
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • raise (child)
  • rear
  • bring up (child)
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Gisigu'sgw pugwelnitl gisneps'tgwa'latl.
    Translation: The old lady raised lots of children
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: nep·sêt·kwaa·la·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • neps'tgwa'l'g -- I raise him/her -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • neps'tgwa'lul -- I raise you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • neps'tgwa'lit -- He/she raises me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
