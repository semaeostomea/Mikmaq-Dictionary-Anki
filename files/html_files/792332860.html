elqamgwejat

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm

Translation: He/she braces self
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • brace to keep balance
  • brace to slow down
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Telgesnuguig ngat amujpa elqamgwejai pata'wti.
    Translation: My foot is so sore I have to brace myself using the table.
