etegjigsma'latl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she pushes him/her backwards
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • push backwards
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: So'sep etegjigsma'latl ugtue'ml.
    Translation: Joseph pushes his horse backwards.
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: e路dek路chik路s锚路maa路la路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • etegjigsma'l'g -- I push him/her backwards -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • etegjigsma'lul -- I push you backwards -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • etegjigsma'lit -- He/she pushes me backwards -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
