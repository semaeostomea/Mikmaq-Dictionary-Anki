wesuisepilasit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: ...is gathered and tied
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • gathered and tied
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ala wesuisepilasit nta'pim gaqapijing.
    Translation: My fishing net is hanging there gathered and tied.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
