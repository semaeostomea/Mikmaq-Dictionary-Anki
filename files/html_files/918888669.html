weltesguatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she meets him/her
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • meet
  • meet up with (on foot)
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Qame'g etlultesgatipnig.
    Translation: They (two) met in Campbellton (across the river).
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: wel·tes·ku·a·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • weltesgaq -- I meet him/her -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • weltesgul -- I meet you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • weltesguit -- He/she meets me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)

Related entries for category "meet"

  • a'sugwesugwijig : meet on water(by canoe) / come together (by rowboat)
  • welta'tl : hit well / meet up with / catch
  • weltesg'g : meet up with (on foot) / meet or arrive on time
  • weltesguatl : meet / meet up with (on foot)
