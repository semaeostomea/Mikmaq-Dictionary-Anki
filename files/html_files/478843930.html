jaqalipnet

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: His/her illness progresses quickly
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • illness progresses quickly
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Jijuaqa mimajuinu maw jaqalipnet.
    Translation: Sometimes a person's illness progresses very quickly.
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: ja路hga路li路b锚路net

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • jaqalipnei -- My illness progresses quickly -- (first person singular animate)
  • jaqalipneieg -- Our illness progresses quickly -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • jaqalipna'tieg -- Our illness progresses quickly -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
