nepasma'sit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she goes to lay down to sleep
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • goes to lay down to sleep
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Nepasma'sisnaq nitapo'q.
    Translation: Looks like my buddy laid down to sleep.
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: ne·ba·sê·maa·sit

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • Nepasma'tieg -- We lay down to sleep -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • Nepismitaieg -- We lay down to sleep -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
Document References:
  • Pacifique Dictionary Manuscript, Page Image #529
    Transcription:(mpo'qon) y aller, nepasmasi
    Transliteration:(mpo'qon) to go, nepasma'si
