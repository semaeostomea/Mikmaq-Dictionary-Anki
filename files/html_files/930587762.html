welo'tmugsit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: His/her possession is treated well (by others)
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • possession(s) treated well (by others)
  • possession(s) well taken care of (by others)
  • possession(s) well looked after (by others)
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Tuma na mawigsatg welo'tmugsij utapsum.
    Translation: Tom appreciates when his possessions are well taken by others.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: wel路oo路d锚m路uk路sit

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • welo'tmugsi -- my possession is treated well (by others) -- (first person singular animate)
  • welo'tmugsieg -- our possession are treated well (by others) -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • welo'tmugsiegl -- our possessions are treated well (by others) -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
