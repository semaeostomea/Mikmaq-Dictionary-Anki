wi'g

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: His/her house
Part of Speech: noun inanimate
Meanings:
  • house
  • dwelling
  • home
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ms't wen welaptmuaj wi'g ta'n teliulugwas'g.
    Translation: Everyone like the way his/her house is decorated.
