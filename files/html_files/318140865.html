ugsuguni

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: His/her tail
Part of Speech: noun inanimate
Meanings:
  • tail
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ntue'm ugsuguni toqwaqji'jg.
    Translation: My dog's tail is short.
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: uk·su·gu·ni

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • ugsuguniwal -- their tails -- (3p(0p))
