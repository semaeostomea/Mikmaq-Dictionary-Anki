sape'wig

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: It is holy
Part of Speech: verb inanimate intransitive
Meanings:
  • holy
  • blessed
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Tegen na'gweg gi'l telta'sin maw sape'wig?
    Translation: What day do you think is most holy?
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: sa路bee路wik

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • sape'wigl -- Those are blessed -- (third person plural inanimate)
