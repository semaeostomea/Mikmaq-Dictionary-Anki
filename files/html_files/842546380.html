eleget

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw

  • 1.

    Translation: He/she throws it
    Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
    Meanings:
    • throw
    • release
    • play (a game)
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Ulo'nug mgumie'jg elagatieg.
      Translation: Tonight we are playing (some games of) horseshoes.
      Recording: Recording by dmm
    Pronunciation Guide: e路le路get

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • elegei -- I throw it -- (first person singular animate)
    • elegeieg -- We throw it -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
    • elaqatieg -- We throw it -- (first person plural exclusive animate)

  • 2.

    Translation: He/she runs for office
    Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
    Meanings:
    • run (for office)
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Eleget ugjit nnu saqamawin.
      Translation: He/she is running for chief.
      Recording: Recording by ewm
    Pronunciation Guide: e路le路get

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • elegei -- I run for office -- (first person singular animate)
    • elegeieg -- We run for office -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
    • elaqatieg -- We run for office -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
