gelapaqte'g

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she tacks it on
Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
Meanings:
  • tack something on
  • attach with nails
  • tack on with nails
  • stitch on or together temporarily
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Gelapaqte'g pe' nige'.
    Translation: He/she attaches it just for now.
