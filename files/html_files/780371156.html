taqawanapi

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Grilse net
Part of Speech: noun animate
Meanings:
  • grilse net
  • trout net
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ge' e'wanej taqawanapi.
    Translation: Let's use the grilse net.
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: da·hga·wa·na·bi

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • taqawanapi'g, -- grilse nets -- (plural)
  • taqawanapi'l -- a grilse net -- (fourth person)
