pasg'sematl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she lights it (tobacco pipe) up
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • light up (tobacco pipe)
  • light up a cigarette
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Gisigu pasg'sematl utmaqann.
    Translation: The old man lights his pipe.
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: bas路k锚路se路ma路d锚l

Related Entry: pasgesematl
Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • pasg'semg -- I light it up -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
