nipiapetesmat

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she cooks overnight for himself/herself
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • cooks overnight for self
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Nipiapetesma'tieg gulaman ma' sespete'tmueg mijipjewei sapo'nug.
    Translation: We are cooking overnight for ourselves so we won't have to worry about (preparing) food tomorrow.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: ni·pi·abe·de·sêm·at

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • nipiapetesmaieg -- We cook overnight for ourselves. -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • nipiapetesma'tieg -- We cook overnight for ourselves. -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
Document References:
  • Pacifique Dictionary Manuscript, Page Image #607
    Transcription:(A. M. nipiapetesemai), faire cuir (riz, etc.) de nuit
    Expanded:(A. M. nipiapetesemai), faire cuire (riz, etc.) de nuit
    Transliteration:(A. M. nipiapetesmai), to cook (rice, etc.) overnight, overnight cooking
    Source as entry:nipiapetesmai, I cook (rice, etc.) overnight for myself
    Normalized source as entry:nipiapetesmat, he/she cooks overnight for himself/herself
