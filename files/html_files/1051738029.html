mawgimatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she includes him/her in the count
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • include in the count
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Mawgimsit telimiteg ta'n tesi'tij.
    Translation: He/she counted him/herself in when he/she told me how many they were.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: maw·ki·ma·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • mawgimg -- I include him/her in the count -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • mawgimul -- I include you in the count -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • mawgimit -- He/she includes me in the count -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
