egtapluit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she hunts
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • hunts
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Gi's aqantie'uti egtapluit aq me' mnaq goqwei.
    Translation: He/she has been hunting for a week and still nothing.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: ek路da路b锚l路u路it

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • egtaplui -- I hunt -- (first person singular animate)
  • egtapluieg -- We hunt -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • egtaplultieg -- We hunt -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
