elpualatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she wants him/her/it there
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • want there
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Na lo'gsl elpualatl wiguwaq.
    Translation: He/she wants that log at his/her house.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: el·bu·a·la·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • elpualg -- el'pualk -- I want him/her/it there -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
