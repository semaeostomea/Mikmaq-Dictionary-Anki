pejijing'ja'latl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she squeezes him/her/it unintentionally
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • squeeze unintentionally
  • squeeze accidently
  • pinch unintentionally
  • pinch accidently
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ge's etlmila'ti'tij ugjignaml, na pejijing'ja'lut.
    Translation: While he/she was playing with his/her brother, he/she got squeezed unintentionally.
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: be路ji路jin路g锚路jaa路la路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • pejijing'ja'l'g -- I squeeze him/her/it unintentionally -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • pejijing'ja'lul -- I squeeze you unintentionally -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • pejijing'ja'lit -- He/she squeezes me unintentionally -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
