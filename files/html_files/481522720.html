menaje'jit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is frail
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • frail
  • fragile
  • delicate
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Nsugwis nugu' menaje'jit.
    Translation: My aunt is quite frail now.
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: me·na·jee·jit

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • menaje'ji -- I am frail -- (first person singular animate)
  • menaje'jieg -- We are frail -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • menaje'jultieg -- We are frail -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
