teluisit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is named
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • named
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Nte'pitem A'tli teluisit.
    Translation: My wife's name is Audrey.
    Recording: Recording by jnw

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • teluisi -- My name is -- (first person singular animate)
  • teluisieg -- Our names are -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • teluisultieg -- Our names are -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
