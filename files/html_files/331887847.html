esnaqamigeg

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: It is solid ground
Part of Speech: verb inanimate intransitive
Meanings:
  • solid ground
  • firm ground
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Pilei awti eteg ta'n esnaqamigeg.
    Translation: The new road is located where the ground is solid.
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: es·na·hka·mi·kek

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • esnaqamigegl -- the grounds are solid -- (third person plural inanimate)
Document References:
  • Pacifique Dictionary Manuscript, Page Image #172
    Transcription:esnagamigeg, terrain solide (genèse)
    Transliteration:esnaqamigeg, solid ground (origin, genesis, creation)
    Public Note:Speakers Group discussion concluded "solid ground" (and "firm ground") was sufficient, and to leave the (genèse) meaning for now because only reported by Pacifique; Rand and Clark versions are for solid and firm ground.
  • Dictionary of the language of the Micmac Indians : who reside in Nova Scotia, New Brunswick, Prince Edward Island, Cape Breton and Newfoundland, Page Image #256
  • Rand's Micmac dictionary from phonographic word-lists - transcribed and alphabetically arranged, with a grammar and list of place-names by Jeremiah S. Clark, Page Image #91
