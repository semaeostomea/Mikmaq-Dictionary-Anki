espa'sit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she/it going up high (mountrain or status)
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • go high
  • go up high
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Mali weliespa'sit ugtlugowaqanmg.
    Translation: Mary is going high at her work.
    Recording: Recording by jnw üîâ
Pronunciation Guide: es¬∑paa¬∑sit

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • espa'si -- I am going high -- (first person singular animate)
  • espa'tieg -- we are going high -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • espitaieg -- we are going high -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
