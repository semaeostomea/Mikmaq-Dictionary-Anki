nsanoqonit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is dangerous
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • dangerous
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ula mesgilg nmu'j nsanoqonit.
    Translation: This big dog is dangerous.
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: ên·sa·no·hgo·nit

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • nsanoqoni -- I am dangerous -- (first person singular animate)
  • nsanoqonieg -- We are dangerous -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • nsanoqonultieg -- We are dangerous -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
