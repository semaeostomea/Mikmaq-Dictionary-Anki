giwto'qopilatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she wraps it (rope) around
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • wrap around
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: A'su'n e'w'g giwto'qopilatl ugtapsute'gann.
    Translation: She uses a blanket to wrap the doll.
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: giw·too·hgo·bi·la·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • giwto'qopilg -- I wrap it around -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
