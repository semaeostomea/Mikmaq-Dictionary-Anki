welo'tmuet

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she treats others and their possessions well
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • treat others and their possessions well
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Mqatu'a's ta'n pas'g goqwei, muta welo'tmuet.
    Translation: I'd lend him/her anything, because he/she treats others possessions well.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: wel·oo·dêm·u·et

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • welo'tmuei -- I treat others and their possessions well -- (first person singular animate)
  • welo'tmueieg -- we treat others and their possessions well -- (2d)
  • welo'tmua'tieg -- we treat others and their possessions well -- (third person plural animate)
