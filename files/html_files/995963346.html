esgoqsit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm

Translation: It is undercooked
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • undercooked
  • left untouched by fire
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Me' gatu esgoqsit ula psaqa'gw!
