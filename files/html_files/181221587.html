nisaqalatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she throws him/her/it down
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • throw down
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Suel nisaqalatl, gatu mesnatl.
    Translation: He/she almost threw it (animate), but caught it in time.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: ni·sa·hga·la·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • nisaqalg -- I throw him/her down -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • nisaqalul -- I throw you down -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • nisaqalit -- He/she throws me down -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
