naji'gat

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is going to build his/her house at (location)
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • going to build own house at (location)
  • going to build own camp at (location)
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Sulian naji'gat nipugtug.
    Translation: Sulian is going to build himself a camp in the woods.
