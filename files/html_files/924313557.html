wannoqsit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: It...is curved inward
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • curved inward
  • concave
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ma'munwannoqsit na ula waltes.
    Translation: That waltes bowl is very concave.
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: wan路nohk路sit
