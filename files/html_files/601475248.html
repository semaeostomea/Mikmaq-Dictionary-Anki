niwa'toq

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she dries it out
Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
Meanings:
  • dry out
  • empties/removes all liquid out
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ugtul gisigtapaqto'gweg, nige' amujgpa niwa'toq.
    Translation: His/her boat has gotten swamped, now he/she has to empty it.
