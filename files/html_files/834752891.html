wetapaqamit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she orginates from
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • orginate from
  • spring from
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Sa'n wetapaqamit Elsipugtug.
    Translation: John is from Elsipugtug.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: we·da·ba·hga·mit

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • wetapaqami -- I orginate from -- (first person singular animate)
  • wetapaqamieg -- We orginate from -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • wetapaqamultieg -- We orginate from -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
