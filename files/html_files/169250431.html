s'gepn

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Wild potato
Part of Speech: noun animate
Meanings:
  • wild potato
  • ground nut
  • Indian potato
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Etug me' na'tami nigwejig s'gepng?
    Translation: Do you think that wild potatoes still grow here somewhere?
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: s锚路ge路b锚n

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • s'gepng -- wild potatoes -- (plural)
  • s'gepnn -- a wild potato -- (fourth person)
