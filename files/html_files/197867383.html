enmigjo'gwet

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is stooped over facing away
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • stooped over facing away
  • bent over with buttocks up
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Gi's pana enmigjo'gwet ala ugtiga'taqang etllugwet.
    Translation: There he/she is stooped over again working in his/her garden.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: en路mik路choo路kwet

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • enmigjo'gwejig -- They are stooped facing away -- (third person dual animate)
  • enmigjo'gutijig -- They are stooped facing away -- (third person plural animate)
