mawisetesg'g

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she crumples it with his/her feet
Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
Meanings:
  • crumple with feet
  • kicks it together
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Mawisetesg'gman nta'su'nl, wesamp'teg.
    Translation: I crumple my blankets, it is too hot.
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: ma·wi·se·des·kêk

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • mawisetesgm -- I crumple it with my feet -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
  • mawisetesgmeg -- We crumple it with our feet -- (first person plural exclusive animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
