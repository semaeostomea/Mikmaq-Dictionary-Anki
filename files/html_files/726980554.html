najipqasa'piet

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is going fishing with a net
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • go fishing with a net
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Etlgisgaja'toq ms't goqwei, najipgasa'piet miawitpa'g.
    Translation: He/she is getting everything ready, he/she is going fishing with a net in the middle of the night.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: na·jip·hga·saa·bi·et

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • najipqasa'piei -- I am going fishing with a net -- (first person singular animate)
  • najipqasa'pieieg -- We are going fishing with a net -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • najipqasa'pia'tieg -- We are going fishing with a net -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
Document References:
  • Pacifique Dictionary Manuscript, Page Image #562
    Transcription:natji pgasapiei, aller pêcher au ret (108, 2) natasmogoei, flambeau
    Transliteration:najipqasa'piei, to go fish with a net, natasmugwei, torch
    Source as entry:natji pgasapiei
    Normalized source as entry:najipqasa'piei
