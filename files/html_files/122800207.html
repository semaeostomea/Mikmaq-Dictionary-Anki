wetmamgewei

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Material for smoking
Part of Speech: noun animate
Meanings:
  • material for smoking
  • pipe
  • tobacco
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Gaqisimilamu'g wetmamgewei gisgug eig.
    Translation: There are many kinds of materials for smoking these days.
    Recording: Recording by ewm

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
