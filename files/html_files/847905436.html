paqaiatgweitesmatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she slams him/her/it down
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • slam down
  • body slam
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Paqaiatgweitesmatl amgwanji'tl aq poqjimajia'qteget.
    Translation: He/she slams down the spoon and begins to scold.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: ba路hgai路at路kwey路de路s锚路ma路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • paqaiatgweitesm'g -- I slam him/her/it down -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • paqaiatgweitesmul -- I slam you down -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • paqaiatgweitesmit -- He/she/it slams me down -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
