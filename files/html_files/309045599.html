mejige'g

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw

  • 1.

    Translation: He/she is dirty
    Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
    Meanings:
    • dirty
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Ge' mna'l na atlai, mejige'g.
      Translation: Take that shirt off, it's dirty.
      Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
    Pronunciation Guide: me·ji·geeg

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • mejige'i -- I am dirty -- (first person singular animate)
    • mejige'ieg -- we are dirty -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
    • mejigo'ltieg -- we are dirty -- (first person plural exclusive animate)

  • 2.

    Translation: It is dirty
    Part of Speech: verb inanimate intransitive
    Meanings:
    • dirty
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Gsispa'tu na pata'wti, mejege'j.
      Translation: Wash that table, it's dirty.
      Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
    Pronunciation Guide: me·ji·geeg

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • mejige'gl -- those are dirty -- (third person plural inanimate)
