poqtawlalatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she lugs him/her/it away
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • lug away
  • carry away
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Lola wenaqa'latl unjann aq poqtawlalatl.
    Translation: Lawrence picks up his child and carries him/her away.
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: bohk路daw路la路la路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • poqtawlalg -- I lug him/her/it away -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • poqtawlalul -- I lug you away -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • poqtawlalit -- He/she lugs me away -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
