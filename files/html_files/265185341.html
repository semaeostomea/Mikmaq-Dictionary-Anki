mesugtaqanat

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she craves
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • crave
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Gisgug mesugtaqanai ugjit plamuei.
    Translation: Today I am craving for salmon.
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: mes·suk·ta·hga·nat

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • mesugtaqanai -- I crave -- (first person singular animate)
  • mesugtaqanaieg -- We crave -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • mesugtaqana'tieg -- We crave -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
