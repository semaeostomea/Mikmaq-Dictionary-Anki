ejigleget

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she throws it away
Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
Meanings:
  • throw away
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Gjitmei ejiglegen na tu'gwesmunei?
    Translation: Are you really throwing out that pillowcase?
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: e路ji路g锚路le路get

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • ejiglegei -- I throw it away -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
  • ejiglegeieg -- We throw it away -- (first person plural exclusive animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
