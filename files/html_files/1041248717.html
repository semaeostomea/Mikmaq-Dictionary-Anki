nemisguatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she gets him/her
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • get
  • fetch
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Pegisin tet, gi'l natnemisgul.
    Translation: I've come here to get you.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: ne·mis·ku·a·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • nemisgaq -- I get him/her -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • nemisgul -- I get you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • nemisguit -- He/she gets me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
