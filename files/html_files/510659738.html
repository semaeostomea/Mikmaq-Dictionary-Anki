gepsaqa'latl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she closes him/her
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • close
  • shut
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Gepsaqa'latl ugpojml aq teluet: mu ugsentmiw nugu'.
    Translation: He/she closes his/her purse and says: I have no more money.
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: gep·sa·hgaa·la·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • gepsaqa'l'g -- I close him/her/it -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
