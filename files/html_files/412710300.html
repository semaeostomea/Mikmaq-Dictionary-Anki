mesna'latl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she mends it(shirt)
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • mend
  • repair
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Carol gismesna'latl ntatlaiml ugjit ni'n.
    Translation: Carol has repaired my shirt for me.
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: me·sê·naa·la·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • mesna'l'g -- I mend it -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
