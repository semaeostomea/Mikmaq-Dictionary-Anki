naqani'get

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she bails out water
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • bail
  • scoop out
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Newtiegsitpu'g gi's wesgowa'sit naqani'get, ma' alam gaqa'tegegw.
    Translation: He/she has been here bailing all morning, he/she won't be finished for a long time.
