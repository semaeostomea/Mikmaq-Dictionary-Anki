naqsilugwet

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she works quickly
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • work quickly
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Agase'wa'lanej Tuma, muta naqsilugwet negm.
    Translation: Let's hire Tom, because he works quickly.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: nahk路si路lu路gwet

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • naqsilugwei -- I work quickly -- (first person singular animate)
  • naqsilugweieg -- we work quickly -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • naqsilugutieg -- we work quickly -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
