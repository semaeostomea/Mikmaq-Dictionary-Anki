wejgwimaqamigewa'sit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she/it comes by land
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • Coming by land.
  • Coming over land.
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Pie'l Muliang wejiet aq wejgwimaqamigewa'sit.
    Translation: Peter is coming from Montreal and he traveling overland.
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: wech路kwi路ma路hga路mi路ge路waa路sit

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • wejgwimaqamigewa'tieg -- We are coming by land. -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • wejgwimaqamigewitaieg -- We are coming by land. -- (plural)
Document References:
  • Pacifique Dictionary Manuscript, Page Image #656
    Transcription:(venir) par terre, oetjgoimagamigeoasi
    Transliteration:(come) by land, wejgwimaqamigewa'si
