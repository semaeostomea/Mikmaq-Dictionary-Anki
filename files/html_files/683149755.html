a'tugwewatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she tells him/her a story
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • tell story to
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Nmijgamij a'tugwewip wela'gwe'g.
    Translation: My grandfather told me a story last night.
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: aa·du·gwe·wa·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • a'tugwewg -- I tell a story to him/her -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • a'tugwewul -- I tell a story to you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • a'tugwewit -- He/she tells a story to me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
