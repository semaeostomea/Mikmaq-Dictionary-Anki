ejigla'latl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she takes him/her away
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • take away
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Goqwei wejijigla'let?
    Translation: Why are you taking him/her away?
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: e路ji路g锚路laa路la路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • ejigla'l'g -- I take him away -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • ejigla'lul -- I take you away -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • ejigla'lit -- He/she takes me away -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
