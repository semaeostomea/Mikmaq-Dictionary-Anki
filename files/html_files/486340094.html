ne'naqpega'toq

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
  • Recording by ___
Translation: He/she lightens it, reduces the load or weight
Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
Meanings:
  • lightens it (ship)
  • reduce the load
  • reduce the weight
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Amuj pana ne'naqpega'tew ugtul mu 'gtu'niwetesinug.
    Translation: He/she will have to lighten his/her boat if he/she doesn't want to get stuck on shore.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: nee路nahk路pe路gaa路tohk

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • ne'naqpega'tu -- I lighten it (ship) -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
  • ne'naqpega'tueg -- we lighten it (ship) -- (first person plural exclusive animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
