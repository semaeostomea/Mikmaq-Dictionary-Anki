unaqapeml

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: His/her disciple
Part of Speech: noun animate
Meanings:
  • disciple
  • follower
  • councillor
  • friend
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Nnu' saqamaw aq unaqapeml pegisingig.
    Translation: The native Chief and his\her councillor have arrived.
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: u路na路hga路be路m锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • unaqapeminaq -- our(x) disciples -- (1x(3p))
  • unaqapemual -- their disciple -- (3p(4))
  • unaqapemua -- their disciples -- (3p(4p))
