tetaqite'lmatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is anxious for him/her
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • anxious for
  • eager for
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Tetaqite'lmatl unjann apaja'sinin.
    Translation: He/she is anxious for his/her child to get back.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: de路da路hgi路dee路l锚路ma路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • tetaqite'lmg, tetaqite'lm'g -- He/she is anxious for him/her -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • tetaqite'lmul -- I am anxious for you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • tetaqite'lmit -- He/she is anxious for me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
