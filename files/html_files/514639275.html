ne'naqa'toq

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she makes a burden/load light.
Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
Meanings:
  • make burden light
  • make load light
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Wasmigsugjaq alawlatmi'tij, amujpa ne'naqa'tu'tuj.
    Translation: Their load they're carrying around is too heavy, they have to lighten it (the load).
