gejimoqteg

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: It is obstructing the way
Part of Speech: verb inanimate intransitive
Meanings:
  • is obstructing the way
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Miamuj natgoqwei gejimoqteg muta mu saputa's'tnug goqwei.
    Translation: There has to be some obstruction because nothing is getting through.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: ge路ji路mohk路tek

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • gejimoqtegl -- They are obstructing the way -- (third person plural inanimate)
