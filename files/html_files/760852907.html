amaligsatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she carves him (statue)
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • carve
  • wittle
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Nape'wl amaligsatl.
    Translation: He carves a rooster.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: a·ma·lik·sa·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • amaligs'g -- I carve him -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
