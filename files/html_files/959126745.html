elamgleg

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Fire burns towards...
Part of Speech: verb inanimate intransitive
Meanings:
  • burn toward
  • burn in a direction
  • burn along a path
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Elamgleg glapis awtigtug, gatu jiptug na'te'l tlinqatest'tew.
    Translation: It burns in the direction to the road, so hopefully it will stop there.
