wejilugwet

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw

  • 1.

    Translation: He/she works because...
    Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
    Meanings:
    • work because
    • reason for work
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Wejilugwet muta gesatg ugtlugowaqan.
      Translation: He/she works because he/she likes his/her work.
      Recording: Recording by dmm
    Pronunciation Guide: we路ji路lu路gwet

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • wejilugwei -- I work because... -- (first person singular animate)
    • wejilugweieg -- we work because... -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
    • wejilugutieg -- we work because... -- (first person plural exclusive animate)

  • 2.

    Translation: He/she works from (location)
    Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
    Meanings:
    • work from (location)
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Ala' na mujga' wane'g wetlugwet.
      Translation: He/she has been working from way down the mission.
      Recording: Recording by jnw
    Pronunciation Guide: we路ji路lu路gwet

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • wejilugwei -- I work from (location) -- (first person singular animate)
    • wejilugweieg -- we work from (location) -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
    • wejilugutieg -- we work from (location) -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
