nipetesmat

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
  • Recording by rem
Translation: He/she cooks (rice, etc.) overnight
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • cooks overnight
  • overnight cooking
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ta'n goqe'nutug etlwisuggwatg na we'gaw nipetesmat.
    Translation: I wonder what he/she is cooking that he/she has to cook it overnight.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: ni路b锚路te路s锚m路at

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • nipetesmaieg -- we cook overnight for ourselves -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • nipetesma'tieg -- we cook overnight for ourselves -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
Document References:
  • Pacifique Dictionary Manuscript, Page Image #607
    Transcription:nipetesemai, faire cuir (riz, etc.) de nuit

    Source as entry:nipetesmai, I cook (rice, etc.) overnight, overnight cooking
    Normalized source as entry:nipetesmat, he/she cooks (rice, etc.) overnight, overnight cooking
