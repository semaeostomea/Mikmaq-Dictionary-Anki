poqwatign

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Umbrella
Part of Speech: noun inanimate
Meanings:
  • umbrella
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Tala's'gsneg ntpoqwatignmeg?
    Translation: Where did my umbrella go?
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: bohk路wa路di路g锚n

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • poqwatignn -- umbrellas -- (plural)
