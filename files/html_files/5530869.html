naqsinpat

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she/it sleeps easily
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • sleep easily
  • sleep without trouble
  • go to sleep early
  • falls asleep quickly
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Naqsinpat, telteto'qwigispnet.
    Translation: He/she falls asleep quickly, he/she is so totally exhausted.
