ginuewei

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw

  • 1.

    Translation: He/she/it is ours (inclusive)
    Part of Speech: unclassified part of speech
    Meanings:
    • ours (inclusive)
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Ula pilei nmu'ji'j ginuowei.
      Translation: This new puppy is ours.
      Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
    Pronunciation Guide: gi·nu·e·wey

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • ginuewe'g -- They are ours -- (third person plural animate)

  • 2.

    Translation: It is ours (inclusive)
    Part of Speech: unclassified part of speech
    Meanings:
    • ours (inclusive)
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Negla wa'wul telte'tmann ginuowe'l.
      Translation: I think those eggs are ours.
      Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
    Pronunciation Guide: gi·nu·e·wey

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • ginuewe'l -- Those are ours -- (third person plural inanimate)
