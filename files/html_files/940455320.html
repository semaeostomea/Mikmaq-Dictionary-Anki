natlutmu'tijig

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: They (ducks) are lured ashore by duck calls
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • ducks lured ashore by duck calls
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Pa'qalaiwig ta'n te'si'tij natlutmu'tijig.
    Translation: I am surprised by how many ducks are lured ashore by the duck calls.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: nat路lu路d锚m路uu路di路jik

Document References:
  • Pacifique Dictionary Manuscript, Page Image #562

    Transliteration:natlutm natlutmutijig, attracted to the shore? the ducks P. Met. say na'taqamultijig see watigei
