alaqsing

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she/it flies around
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • fly around
  • flying
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Talui'tut na jipji'j alaqsing?
    Translation: What kind of bird is that flying around?
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: a路lahk路sink

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • alaqsin -- I fly around -- (first person singular animate)
  • alaqsineg -- we fly around -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • alaqsultieg -- we fly around -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
Document References:
  • Pacifique Dictionary Manuscript, Page Image #10
    Transcription:alagsin, soltitjig, voler, voltiger (15,1)
    Expanded:alagsin, alagsoltitjig, voler, voltiger
    Transliteration:alaqsin, alaqsultijig, fly, flutter, flit (15,1)
  • Rand's Micmac dictionary from phonographic word-lists - transcribed and alphabetically arranged, with a grammar and list of place-names by Jeremiah S. Clark, Page Image #53
