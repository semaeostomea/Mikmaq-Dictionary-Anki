atuasgweitesing

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she falls backwards
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • fall backwards
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ngwis atuasgweitesing msaqtaqtug.
    Translation: My son falls backwarks on the floor.
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: a·du·as·kwey·de·sink

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • atuasgweitesin -- I fall backwards -- (first person singular animate)
  • atuasgweitesineg -- We fall backwards -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • atuasgweitesultieg -- We fall backwards -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
