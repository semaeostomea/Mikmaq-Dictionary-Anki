gisteluatl

Translation: He impregnated her (slang expression)
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • impregnate (slang expression)
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Mu' gejiam'g ta'n tegen nmu'j gisteluaqtl nti'l.

Pronunciation Guide: gis·te·lu·a·dêl

  • gisteluit -- He impregnated me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
