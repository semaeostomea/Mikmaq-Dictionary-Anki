apgnualatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is pacifying someone
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • pacify
  • soothe
  • calm
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Apgnualatl unjanji'jl.
    Translation: He/She pacifies his/her child.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: ap路g锚路nu路a路la路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • apgnualg -- apknualk -- I pacify him/her -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • apgnualul -- apknualul -- I pacify you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • apgnualit -- apknualit -- He/she pacifies me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
