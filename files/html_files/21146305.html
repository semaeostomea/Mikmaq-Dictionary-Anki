igalatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she defends him/her
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • protect
  • defend
  • fight for
  • take side of
  • favor
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Sulian igalatl uggwe'ji'jl.
    Translation: William defends his younger sister.
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: i·ga·la·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • igalg -- I defend him/her -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • igalul -- I defend you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • igalit -- He/she defends me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
