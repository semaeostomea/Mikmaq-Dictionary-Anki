wissuignetg

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she overpowers it
Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
Meanings:
  • overpower
  • overcome
  • conquer
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Glapis gatu wissuignetgeg ta'n goqwei ne'gaw wesgweiaj.
    Translation: He/she finally overcame what's been bugging him/her all along.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: wis·su·i·gê·netk

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • wissuignetm -- I overpower it -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
  • wissuignetmeg -- We overpower it -- (first person plural exclusive animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
