jipji'ju'gisutnat

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she has a voice like a bird
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • have voice like bird
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Gajuewjig alulgwalji muta jipji'ju'gisutnat.
    Translation: Cats follow him/her around because he/she has a voice like a bird.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: jip·chii·juu·gi·su·dê·nat

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • jipji'ju'gisutnai -- I have a voice like a bird -- (first person singular animate)
  • jipji'ju'gisutnaieg -- We have voices like a bird -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • jipji'ju'gisutna'tieg -- We have voices like a bird -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
