salitewa'teget

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she gives for charity
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • give charity
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Pas'g Nuelewumg to'q nitap salite'wa'tegetew.
    Translation: Only at Christmas does my friend give to charity.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: sa·li·de·waa·de·get

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • salitewa'tegei -- I give for charity -- (first person singular animate)
  • salitewa'tegeieg -- We give for charity -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • salitewa'tega'tieg -- We give for charity -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
Borrowed Word: from French, charité
