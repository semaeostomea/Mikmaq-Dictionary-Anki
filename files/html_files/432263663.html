mesni'sas'g

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: It is patched
Part of Speech: verb inanimate intransitive
Meanings:
  • patched
  • mended
  • sewn
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Te's na siggw pas'g mesni'smi'titl ntawtinal.
    Translation: Every spring they only patch our roads.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: me·sê·nii·sa·sêk

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • mesni'sas'gl -- They are patched -- (third person plural inanimate)
