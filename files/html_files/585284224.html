tetpaqapuguit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she/it is standing straight
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • stands straight
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Tetpaqapuguit nige', napui'g ge's mu ap nisiegw.
    Translation: It is standing straight now, take the photograph before it falls again.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: det·pa·hga·bu·gu·id

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • tetpaqapugui -- I am standing straight -- (first person singular animate)
  • tetpaqapuguieg -- We are standing straight -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • tetpaqapugultieg -- We are standing straight -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
