pesga'qiputeg

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: It is chafed
Part of Speech: verb inanimate intransitive
Meanings:
  • chafed
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Pesga'qiputeg gmu'j ta'n nastestmgewei eteg.
    Translation: The wood is worn off around the hook .
    Recording: Recording by dmm
