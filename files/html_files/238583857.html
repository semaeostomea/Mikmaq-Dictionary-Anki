pesqojiguet

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is sticky
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • sticky
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Amuj gi's sa'q tltmutugunn na suitisl, lpa ms't tami pesqojiguet na lpa'tuj.
    Translation: That little boy must have been eating candies for a long time, he is sticky everywhere.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: bes路hko路ji路gu路et

Related Entry: oqosgijiguet
Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • pesqojiguei -- I am sticky -- (first person singular animate)
  • pesqojigueieg -- we are sticky -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • pesqojigua'tieg -- we are sticky -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
