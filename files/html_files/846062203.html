sa'se'wi'gatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she changes the writing on him/her
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • changes the writing
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Sa'se'wi'g'g gnugwatign, jiptug me' nige' nenuaten.
    Translation: I am changing the writing for this letter, perhaps now it will be more recognizable.
    Recording: Recording by dmm

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
