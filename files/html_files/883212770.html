ugpitnoqom

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: His/her arm
Part of Speech: noun inanimate
Meanings:
  • arm
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Tela'qal ugpitnoqonml, miamuj melgignat.
    Translation: His/her arms are so large (in girth), he/she must be strong.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: uk·pi·dê·nohk·om

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • npitnoqonml -- my arms -- (1(0p))
  • npitnoqonminal -- our(x) arms -- (1x(0p))
  • ugpitnoqomual -- their arms -- (3p(0p))
