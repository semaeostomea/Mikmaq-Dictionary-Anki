siawa'latl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she keeps on going with him/her
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • keeps on going with
  • continue with
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Siawa'latl Ugpiganjig witapl.
    Translation: He/she continues to Eel River Bar taking his/her friend.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: si·a·waa·la·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • siawa'l'g -- I keep on going with him/her -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • siawa'lul -- I keep on going with you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • siawa'lit -- He/she keeps on going with me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
