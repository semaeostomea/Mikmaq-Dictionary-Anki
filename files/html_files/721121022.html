poqtewo'gwet

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she begins to speak
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • begin to speak
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Nuji'j pas'g tapuipuna't aq gi's poqtewo'gwet.
    Translation: My grandchild is just two years old and he/she is already begining to speak.
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: bohk·te·woo·gwet

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • poqtewo'gwei -- I begin to speak -- (first person singular animate)
  • poqtewo'gweieg -- We begin to speak -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • poqtewo'gutieg -- We begin to speak -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
