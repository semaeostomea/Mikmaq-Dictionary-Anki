amjaqa'latl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she smeers someone
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • smeer
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Amjaqa'latl Jonial sisgu.
    Translation: He/she smears Johnny with mud.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: am·cha·hgaa·la·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • amjaqa'l'g -- I smeer someone -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • amjaqa'lul -- I smeer you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • amjaqa'lit -- He smeers me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
