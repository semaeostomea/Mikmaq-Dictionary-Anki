nenatuat

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
  • Recording by rem
Translation: He/she spears/feels around in the mud for eels.
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • feels around in the mud for eels
  • spears eels in the mud
Example of word used in a sentence:
Pronunciation Guide: ne·na·du·at

Related Entry: nantuat
Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • nenatuai -- I spear/feel around in the mud for eels -- (first person singular animate)
  • nenatuaieg -- we spear/feel around in the mud for eels -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • nenatua'tieg -- we spear/feel around in the mud for eels -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
Document References:
  • Pacifique Dictionary Manuscript, Page Image #580
    Transcription:nenatoăi, toăt, tōtitjig, (v. nāntoai), chercher avec le harpon (115, 2)
    Expanded:nenatoăi, nenatoăt, nenatōtitjig, (voir nāntoai), chercher avec le harpon (115, 2)
    Transliteration:nenatuai, nenatuat, nenatu’tijig (see na’ntuai), to search with a harpoon (115, 2)
