nasapaqtesing

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she entangles
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • entangle
  • get caught on
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ge's nti etlmila'sit na nasapaqtesing apa'pigtug.
    Translation: While my dog was playing, he got entangled by the rope.
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: na路sa路bahk路te路sink

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • nasapaqtesin -- I get entangled -- (first person singular animate)
  • nasapaqtesineg -- We get entangled -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • nasapaqtesultieg -- We get entangled -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
