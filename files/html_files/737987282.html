amsalapuguet

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she says something wrong or incorrect
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • say something wrong
  • say something incorrect
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Teleiaq lpa mu me'tegw muta jipatg etug amsalapuguen.
    Translation: I fine he/she is not saying anyting because he/she may be afraid of saying the wrong thing.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: am路sa路lap路bu路gu路et

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • amsalapuguei -- I say something wrong or incorrect -- (first person singular animate)
  • amsalapugueieg -- We say something wrong or incorrect -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • amsalapugua'tieg -- We say something wrong or incorrect -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
