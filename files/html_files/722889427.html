wejipsetamit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is sniffing
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • sniff
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Nmu'j tmk wejipsetamit gesgmnaq pisgwa'gw wenji'guom.
    Translation: The dog first sniffs before going into a house.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: we路jip路se路da路mit

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • wejipsetami -- I am sniffing -- (first person singular animate)
  • wejipsetamieg -- We are sniffing -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • wejipsetamultieg -- We are sniffing -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
