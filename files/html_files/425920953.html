maqigtulit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she has a big boat
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • have a big boat
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Mu telta'siwas telmaqigtulit
    Translation: I didn't think his/her boat was so big.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: ma路hgik路tu路lit

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • maqigtuli -- I have a big boat -- (first person singular animate)
  • maqigtulieg -- We have big boats -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • maqigtulultieg -- We have big boats -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
