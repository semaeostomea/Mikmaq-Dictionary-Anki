ennmtesmatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she sticks it into something by force
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • throw to penetrate into
  • stick (knife) into by force
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Lpa ennmtesmatl witapal wastewigtug.
    Translation: He/she drove his/her friend into the snow.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: en·nêm·te·sê·ma·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • ennmtesmg -- I stick in by force -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
