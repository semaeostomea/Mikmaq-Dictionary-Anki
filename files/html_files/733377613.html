pemang

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is in the process of doing away with it
Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
Meanings:
  • process of doing away with
  • process of destroying
  • process of killing
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Mawiansue'g wen goqwei pemang.
    Translation: It is always sad when someone is in the process of destroying something.
