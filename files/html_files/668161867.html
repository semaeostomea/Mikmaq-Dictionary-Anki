ugpitni'sewet

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she sews by hand
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • sew (by hand)
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Etlingutinpitni'sewet.
    Translation: He/she is sewing by hand by himself/herself.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: uk·pi·dê·nii·se·wet

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • ugpitni'sewei -- I sew by hand -- (first person singular animate)
  • ugpitni'seweieg -- We sew by hand -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • ugpitni'sewa'utieg -- We sew by hand -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
