gi'gajie'watl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she uses it forcefully
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • use in spite of
  • use in spite of condition
  • use forcefully
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Gi'gajie'watl ugtlawo'qoml tlia nige' gesp'nnij.
    Translation: He/she uses his/her knife inspite of it being dull.
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: gii·ga·ji·ee·wa·dêl
