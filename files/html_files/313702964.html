wisgwia'teget

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw

  • 1.

    Translation: He/she plays the bagpipes
    Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
    Meanings:
    • play bagpipes
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Na ji'nm etlwisgwia'teget, Unma'gig na tleiawit.
      Translation: That man playing the bagpipes, he's from Cape Breton.
      Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
    Pronunciation Guide: wis·kwi·aa·de·get

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • wisgwia'tegei -- I play the bagpipes -- (first person singular animate)
    • wisgwia'tegeieg -- We play the bagpipes -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
    • wisgwia'tega'tieg -- We play the bagpipes -- (first person plural exclusive animate)

  • 2.

    Translation: Bagpiper
    Part of Speech: noun animate
    Meanings:
    • bagpiper
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Nemi's'p tami wisgwia'teget, algwilaq?
      Translation: Did you see the bagpiper anywhere, I'm looking for him/her?
      Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
    Pronunciation Guide: wis·kwi·aa·de·get
