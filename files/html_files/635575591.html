mena'gigwet

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm

Translation: He/she pulls out weeds
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • pull out weeds
  • pull up vegetables
Example of word used in a sentence:
