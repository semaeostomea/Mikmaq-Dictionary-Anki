lesqe'gn

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw

  • 1.

    Translation: Trunk
    Part of Speech: noun inanimate
    Meanings:
    • clothes trunk
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Lesqe'gn teluegetmg ugjit aligew.
      Translation: A trunk is used for clothing.
      Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
    Pronunciation Guide: les·hkee·gên

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • lesqe'gnn -- trunks -- (plural)

  • 2.

    Translation: Coffin
    Part of Speech: noun inanimate
    Meanings:
    • coffin
    • casket
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Ala ji'nm nujitoqol lesqe'gnn.
      Translation: That man makes coffins.
      Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
    Pronunciation Guide: les·hkee·gên

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • lesqe'gnn -- coffins -- (plural)
