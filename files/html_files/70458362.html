wi'guamatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she finds someone amusing
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • find amusing
  • laugh at
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Wi'guamatl etliewsitutmnij.
    Translation: He/she finds him/her amusing as he/she acts silly.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: wii路gu路a路ma路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • wi'guamg -- I find someone amusing -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • wi'guamul -- I find you amusing -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • wi'guamit -- He/she finds me amusing -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
