we'ji'wet

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm

Translation: She gives birth
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • give birth
  • bear child (said of woman when she bears child)
Example of word used in a sentence:
