getmete'gl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw

  • 1.

    Translation: He/she wins all of them
    Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
    Meanings:
    • win all
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Lpa ms't getmete'gl Jonni.
      Translation: Johnny wins every single one of them.
      Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
    Pronunciation Guide: ge·dê·me·dee·gêl

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • getmete'mann -- I won them all -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
    • getmete'megl -- We won them all -- (first person plural exclusive animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)

  • 2.

    Translation: He/she breaks all of them
    Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
    Meanings:
    • break all
    • destroy all
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Geget getmete'gl na tuop'ti'l ge's guntewte'g na wenji'guom.
      Translation: He/she has almost broken all of the windows from throwing rocks at that house.
      Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
    Pronunciation Guide: ge·dê·me·dee·gêl

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • getmete'mann -- I broke them all -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
    • getmete'megl -- We broke them all -- (first person plural exclusive animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
