putualatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she blows on him/her
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • blow on
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Tmg putualatl epsinitl tap'tann gesgmnaq malqmagw.
    Translation: First he/she blows on the hot potato before he/she eats it.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: bu路du路a路la路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • putualg -- I blow on him/her -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • putualul -- I blow on you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • putualit -- He/she blows on me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
