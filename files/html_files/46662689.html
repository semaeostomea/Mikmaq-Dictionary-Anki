elaqalatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw

  • 1.

    Translation: He/she throws it toward
    Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
    Meanings:
    • hurl
    • throw toward (ball)
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Musigisg'tug elaqalatl tu'aqann.
      Translation: He/she throws the ball up in the air.
      Recording: Recording by dmm
    Pronunciation Guide: e路la路hga路la路d锚l

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • elaqalg -- I throw him/her/it toward -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)

  • 2.

    Translation: He/she lets go of him/her
    Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
    Meanings:
    • let's go of
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Pegije'g'p we'gaw gisielaqalgeg ntus amgwes poqjiallega'teg.
      Translation: It took long to let go of my daughter when she first started walking.
      Recording: Recording by jnw
    Pronunciation Guide: e路la路hga路la路d锚l

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • elaqalg -- I let go of him/her/it -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
