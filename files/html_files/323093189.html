nata'matnagget

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she knows how to fight
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • know how to fight
  • good fighter
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Nutaigw matnaggewinu ta'n nata'matnagget.
    Translation: We need a fighter who knows how to fight.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: na路daa路ma路d锚路nak路ket

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • nata'matnaggei -- I know how to fight -- (first person singular animate)
  • nata'matnaggeieg -- We know how to fight -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • nata'matnagg'tieg -- We know how to fight -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
