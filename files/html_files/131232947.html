pijigwalatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she presses something into(container)
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • press into(container)
  • squeeze into(container)
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: E'pite'ji'j pijigwalatl ugtapsute'gann muntigtug aq maja'sit.
    Translation: The young girl squeezes her doll into the bag and leaves.
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: bi·ji·gwa·la·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • pijigwalg -- I press him/her/it into(container) -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
