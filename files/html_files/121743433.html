nipinigatne'get

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
  • Recording by rem
Translation: He/she does night racing
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • night racing
  • night races
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Nti na gelu'sit ugjit nipinigatne'gej.
    Translation: My dog is good at night racing.
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: ni·pi·ni·ka·tên·ee·get

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • nipinigatne'geieg -- we race at night -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • njipinigatne'g'tieg, njipinigatne'ga'tieg -- we race at night -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
Document References:
  • Pacifique Dictionary Manuscript, Page Image #607
    Transcription:nipinigatnègei, faire à la course d. n.
    Expanded:nipinigatnègei, faire à la course de nuit
    Transliteration:nipinigatne'gei, do night racing
    Source as entry:nipinigatne'gei, I do night racing
    Normalized source as entry:nipinigatne'get, he/she does night racing, he/she night races
  • Dictionary of the language of the Micmac Indians : who reside in Nova Scotia, New Brunswick, Prince Edward Island, Cape Breton and Newfoundland, Page Image #192
