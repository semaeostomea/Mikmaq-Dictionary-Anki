nipapultimg

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Wake or vigil of the dead
Part of Speech: noun inanimate
Meanings:
  • wake of the dead
  • vigil for the dead/deceased
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Nipapultimg na epituj npuinu newtitpa'q.
    Translation: Nipapultimg is to spend overnight at a wake.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: ni路ba路bul路timk

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • nipapultimgl -- wakes of the dead, vigils for the dead/deceased -- (plural)
