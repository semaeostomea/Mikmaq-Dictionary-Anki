ugsitunapi'l

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: His/her vocal cords
Part of Speech: noun inanimate
Meanings:
  • vocal cords
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Pestaqa'la's nsitunapi'g ugsamsesgweian.
    Translation: I going to strain my vocal cords if I keep yelling.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: uk路si路du路na路piil

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • nsitunapi'g -- my vocal cords -- (1(0p))
  • nsitunapiminaq -- our(x) vocal cords -- (1x(0p))
  • ugsitunapimua, ugsitunapimuaq -- their vocal cords -- (3p(0p))
