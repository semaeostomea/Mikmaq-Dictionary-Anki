gesga'toq

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw

  • 1.

    Translation: He/she makes it disappear
    Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
    Meanings:
    • make disappear
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Newt epa'sit na gesga'toq petaqan.
      Translation: At one sitting, he/she made the pie disappear.
      Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
    Pronunciation Guide: ges·kaa·dohk

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • gesga'tu -- I make it disappear -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
    • gesga'tueg -- We make it disappear -- (first person plural exclusive animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)

  • 2.

    Translation: He/she makes it wider
    Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
    Meanings:
    • make wider
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Wen gesga'toqs na awti.?
      Translation: Who is widening that road?
      Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
    Pronunciation Guide: ges·kaa·dohk

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • gesga'tu -- I make it wider -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
    • gesga'tueg -- We make it wider -- (first person plural exclusive animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
