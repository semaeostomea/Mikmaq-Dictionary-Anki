gomgwejui'gasit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw

  • 1.

    Translation: Sucker fish (gomgwej) writing
    Part of Speech: noun animate
    Meanings:
    • hieroglyphic writing
    • sucker fish (gomgwej) writing
    • Nnu glyph writing
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Nata'gimjig gomgweju'igasijig?
      Translation: Do you read Nnu glyphs?
      Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
    Pronunciation Guide: gom·kwe·ju·ii·ga·sit

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • gomgwejui'gasijig -- sucker fish (gomgwej) writings -- (dual)
    • gomgwejui'gasultijig -- sucker fish (gomgwej) writings -- (plural)
    • gomgwejui'gasinitl -- they are written in sucker fish (gomgwej) writing -- (fourth person)

  • 2.

    Translation: It is written in sucker fish (gomgwej) writing
    Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
    Meanings:
    • written in sucker fish (gomgwej) writing
    • written in Nnu glyph writing
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Ala nespnanej ta'n gigtoqwigomgweju'igasit.
      Translation: Let's bring the one that has Nnu glyphs written around its circumference.
      Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
    Pronunciation Guide: gom·kwe·ju·ii·ga·sit

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • gomgwejui'gasijig -- they are written in sucker fish (gomgwej) writing -- (third person dual animate)
    • gomgwejui'gasultijig -- they are written in sucker fish (gomgwej) writing -- (third person plural animate)
