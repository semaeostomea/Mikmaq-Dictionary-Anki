nestuapuguet

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she speaks wisely or truthfully
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • speak truthfully
  • speak wisely
  • speak intelligently
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Nestuapuguet, etnana wetjigs'taq.
    Translation: He/she makes sense, that is why I heed his/her advise.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: nes路tu路a路bu路gu路et

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • nestuapuguei -- I speak wisely or truthfully -- (first person singular animate)
  • nestuapugueieg -- We speak wisely or truthfully -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • nestuapugua'tieg -- We speak wisely or truthfully -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
