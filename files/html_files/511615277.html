wape'ji'jg

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: It is white and small
Part of Speech: verb inanimate intransitive
Meanings:
  • white small inanimate
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Nutepaqanji'j wape'ji'jg.
    Translation: My little sled is white.
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: wa路bee路jiichk

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • wape'ji'jgl -- They are white and small -- (third person plural inanimate)
