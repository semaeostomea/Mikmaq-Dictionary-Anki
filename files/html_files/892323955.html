te'pineglewei

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Vinegar
Part of Speech: noun inanimate
Meanings:
  • vinegar
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Nugumijaq Llui'so'q mawmilie'w'g'pneg te'pineglewei.
    Translation: My grandmother Louise would use vinegar in many different ways.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: dee·bi·ne·gê·le·wey

Borrowed Word: from French, des_vinaigres
