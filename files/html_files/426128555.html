nessutmalsewet

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she interprets
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • interprete
  • translate
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Natnmi njignam, negm na nessutmalsewet.
    Translation: Go see my brother, he's the one who interprets.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: nes·su·dê·mal·se·wet

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • nessutmalsewei -- I interpret -- (first person singular animate)
  • nessutmalseweieg -- we interpret -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • nessutmalsewa'tieg -- we interpret -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
