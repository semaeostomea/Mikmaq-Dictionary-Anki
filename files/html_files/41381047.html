natawa'qa'latl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is competent in handling it(horse)
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • skillful in handling
  • competent in doing
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Pie'l natawa'qa'latl ugte'sipowml
    Translation: Peter is skillful in handling his horse.
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: na·da·waa·hgaa·la·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • natawa'qa'l'g -- I am competent in handling him/her/it -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • natawa'qa'lul -- I am competent in handling you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • natawa'qa'lit -- He/she is competent in handling me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
