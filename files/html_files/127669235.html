ntugsuinu

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Hunter
Part of Speech: noun animate
Meanings:
  • hunter
  • provider
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Sapo'nug egsitpugwia'titaq ntugsuinu'g najintantaqati'tij.
    Translation: Tomorrow the hunters will be up early to go hunting.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: 锚n路tuk路su路i路nu

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • ntugsuinu'g -- hunters -- (plural)
  • ntugsuinu'l -- a hunter -- (fourth person)
Document References:
  • Pacifique Dictionary Manuscript, Page Image #598
    Transcription:ntogsoino, chasseur
    Transliteration:ntugsuinu, hunter
  • Dictionary of the language of the Micmac Indians : who reside in Nova Scotia, New Brunswick, Prince Edward Island, Cape Breton and Newfoundland, Page Image #150
