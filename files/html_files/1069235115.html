eloqomgiatg

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she fits a handle on something
Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
Meanings:
  • fit handle on
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ta'nug eloqomgiatmn ula ligpenigen?
    Translation: When are you going to put a handle on this basket?
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: e路lo路hgom路ki路atk

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • eloqomgiatm -- I fit a handle on it -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
  • eloqomgiatmeg -- We fit a handle on it -- (first person plural exclusive animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
