maqtawe'g

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw

  • 1.

    Translation: He/she/it is black
    Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
    Meanings:
    • black
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Talamu'g na, maqtawe'g gisna maqtawiewne'g?
      Translation: What color is that, is it black or is it navy blue?
      Recording: Recording by dmm
    Pronunciation Guide: mahk路ta路weeg

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • maqtawe'i -- I am black -- (first person singular animate)
    • maqtawe'ieg -- We are black -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
    • maqtawoltieg -- We are black -- (first person plural exclusive animate)

  • 2.

    Translation: It is black
    Part of Speech: verb inanimate intransitive
    Meanings:
    • black
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Gsatmug maqtawe'g's nutepaqan.
      Translation: I would like it if my car was colored black.
      Recording: Recording by jnw
    Pronunciation Guide: mahk路ta路weeg

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • maqtawe'gl -- They are black -- (third person plural inanimate)
