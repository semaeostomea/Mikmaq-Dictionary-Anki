pugsaqte'gn

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Cedar shingle
Part of Speech: noun inanimate
Meanings:
  • cedar shingle
  • wooden shingle
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Pugsaqte'gna'tas'g wenji'guom mawuliangamgug.
    Translation: A cedar shingled house has a great look.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: buk·sa·hga·dee·gên

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • pugsaqte'gnn -- cedar shingles -- (plural)
