enmigimatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she sends someone home
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • send home
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Wen enmigimt gisgug?
    Translation: Who are you sending home today?
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: en·mi·gi·ma·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • enmigimg -- I send someone home -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • enmigimul -- I send you home -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • enmigimit -- He/she sends me home -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
