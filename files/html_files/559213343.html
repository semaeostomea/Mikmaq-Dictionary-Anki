waqaiewiaq

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: It is filled with pus
Part of Speech: verb inanimate intransitive
Meanings:
  • filled with pus
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ango'te'n, muta waqaiewiaq me' pgije'gt'tew ge's mu nijginug.
    Translation: Be careful, becuase if it fills with pus it will take longer to heal.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: wa路hgay路e路wi路ahk

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • waqaiewiaql -- They are filled with pus -- (third person plural inanimate)
