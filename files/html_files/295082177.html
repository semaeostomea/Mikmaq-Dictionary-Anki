toqju'tesguatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she chases someone up
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • chase up
  • chase upstairs
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Lpa nemi't, toqju'tesguatl gmtnigtug.
    Translation: You could just see him/her/it, chasing him/her/it up the mountain.
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: dohk·chuu·des·ku·a·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • toqju'tesgaq -- I chase someone up -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • toqju'tesgul -- I chase you up -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • toqju'tesguit -- He/she chases me up -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
