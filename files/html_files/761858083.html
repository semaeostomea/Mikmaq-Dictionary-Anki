wettepna'tl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she tries to catch up with him/her/it
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • try to catch up with
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Gina'sit pemtugwi'g wettepna'tl witapal.
    Translation: He/she is running trying hard to catch up with his/her friend.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: wet路te路b锚路naa路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • wettepne'g -- I try to catch up with him/her/it -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • wettepno'l -- I try to catch up with you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • wettepne'lit -- He/she tries to catch up with me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
