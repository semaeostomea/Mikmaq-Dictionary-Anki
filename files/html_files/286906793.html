siwiesgmat

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is tired of waiting
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • tired of waiting
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Pas'g teluep siwiesgmai aq maja'sipnaq.
    Translation: All he/she said was I'm tired of waiting and then he/she left.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: si路wi路es路k锚路mat

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • siwiesgmai -- I am tired of waiting -- (first person singular animate)
  • siwiesgmaieg -- We are tired of waiting -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • siwiesgma'tieg -- We are tired of waiting -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
