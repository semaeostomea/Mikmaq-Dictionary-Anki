sinqamistaqanei

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm

Translation: Strainer
Part of Speech: noun inanimate
Meanings:
  • strainer
  • sieve
  • colander
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ge' jugwa'tui sinqamistaqanei.
    Translation: Would you bring me the strainer.
    Recording: Recording by ewm
