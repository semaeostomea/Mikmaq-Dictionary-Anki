lasguaw

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Showshoe
Part of Speech: noun animate
Meanings:
  • snowshoe
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Lasguaq na pegwatelagig tqo'nug.
    Translation: I will buy snowshoes in the fall.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: las路ku路aw

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • lasguaq -- snowshoes -- (plural)
  • lasguawl -- a snowshoe -- (fourth person)
