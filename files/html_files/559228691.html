talamu'g

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: What color is it?
Part of Speech: verb inanimate intransitive
Meanings:
  • what color?
  • what kind?
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Talamu'g na mutputi?
    Translation: What colour is the chair?/What kind of chair is it?
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: da路la路muug

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • talamu'gl -- what color are they? -- (third person plural inanimate)
