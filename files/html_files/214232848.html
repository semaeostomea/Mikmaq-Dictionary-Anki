gopitewe'get

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she traps beaver
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • trap beaver
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Newtipug pas'g telugwet gopitewe'get.
    Translation: The whole winter the only he/she did was trap beaver.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: go路bi路de路wee路get

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • gopitewe'gei -- kopitewe'key -- I trap beaver -- (first person singular animate)
  • gopitewe'geieg -- kopitewe'keiek -- We trap beaver -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • gopitewe'ga'tieg -- kopitewe'ka'tiek -- We trap beaver -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
Document References:
  • Pacifique Dictionary Manuscript, Page Image #598
    Transcription:gopiteoegei, ch. castor
    Expanded:gopiteoegei, chasser castor
    Transliteration:gopitewe'gei, hunt beaver
