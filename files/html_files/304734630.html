nugwaltugo'sit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she combs his/her hair
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • comb hair
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ge's mu tami eliegw, na nugwaltugo'sit aq plu'jaqamat.
    Translation: Before he/she goes anywhere, he/she combs his/her hair and looks in the mirror.
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: nu·gwal·tu·goo·sit

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • nugwaltugo'si -- I comb my hair -- (first person singular animate)
  • nugwaltugo'sieg -- We comb our hair -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • nugwaltugo'sultieg -- We comb our hair -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
