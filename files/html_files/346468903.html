etltesing

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw

  • 1.

    Translation: He/she is struck there
    Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
    Meanings:
    • where one got struck
    • where one got injured
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Ugjigung etltesing.
      Translation: He/she injured him/her knee.
      Recording: Recording by dmm
    Pronunciation Guide: e路d锚ll路de路sink

  • 2.

    Translation: He/she is moving rythmically
    Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
    Meanings:
    • beat or move rythmically
    • dance
    • shake
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Nata'tesing ala ji'nm etltesing.
      Translation: That man dancing over there dances well.
      Recording: Recording by ewm
    Pronunciation Guide: e路d锚ll路de路sink

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • etltesin -- etl'tesin -- I am moving rythmically -- (first person singular animate)
    • etltesineg -- etl'tesinek -- We are moving rythmically -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
    • etltesultieg -- etl'tesultiek -- We are moving rythmically -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
