welg'g

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she fits it well
Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
Meanings:
  • good fit
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Nte'pitem welg'g na pilei ugtoqon.
    Translation: My wife fits her new dress well.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: wel·kêk

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • welgm -- I fit it well -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
  • welgmeg -- We fit it well -- (first person plural exclusive animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
