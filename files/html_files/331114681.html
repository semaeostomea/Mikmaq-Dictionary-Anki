gisu'g'g

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is warmly dressed
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • warmly dressed
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Gewjiap ulagu, gatu gisu'gm gisgug.
    Translation: I was cold yesterday, so I'm warmly dressed today.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: gis路uu路g锚k

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • gisu'gm -- I am warmly dressed -- (first person singular animate)
  • gisu'gmeg -- We are warmly dressed -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • gisu'gmu'tieg -- We are warmly dressed -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
