amig'pistaqanat

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is somewhat hard of hearing
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • slightly deaf
  • somewhat hard of hearing
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ngij amig'pistaqanat.
    Translation: My mother is slightly deaf.
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: a·mi·gê·bis·ta·hgê·nat

Related Entry: gepistaqanat
Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • amig'pistaqanai -- I am slightly deaf -- (first person singular animate)
  • amig'pistaqanaieg -- We are slightly deaf -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • amig'pistaqana'tieg -- We are slightly deaf -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
