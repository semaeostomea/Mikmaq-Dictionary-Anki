pepga'toq

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she thins it out/down
Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
Meanings:
  • thin out
  • thin down
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ni'n tlueg me' gijga' pepga'tu.
    Translation: I would say thin it out a bit more.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: bep路kaa路dohk

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • pepga'tu -- I thin it out -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
  • pepga'tueg -- We thin it out -- (first person plural exclusive animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
