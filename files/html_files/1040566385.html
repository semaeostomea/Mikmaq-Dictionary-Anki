na'tala'latl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she does something to him/her
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • does something to
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Wen gisna'tala'latl nti'l?
    Translation: Who did something to my dog?
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: naa路dal路aa路la路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • na'tala'l'g -- I do something to him/her -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • na'tala'lul -- I do something to you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • na'tala'lit -- He/she does something to me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
