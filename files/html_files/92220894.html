mui'newqamigsit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm

Translation: He/she has a temperament of a bear
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:

Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Njignam So'sep Nuel na maw mui'newqamigsit.
    Translation: My younger brother Joseph Noel has the temperament of the bear.
