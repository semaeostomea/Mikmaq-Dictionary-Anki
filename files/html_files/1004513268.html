elutuatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she imitates someone
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • impersonate
  • imitate
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ngwis elutuatl mui'nal.
    Translation: My son imitates the bear.
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: e·lu·du·a·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • elutaq -- I imitate someone -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • elutul -- I imitate you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • elutuit -- He/she imitates me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
