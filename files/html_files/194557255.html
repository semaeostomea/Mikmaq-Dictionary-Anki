malpale'wit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Doctor
Part of Speech: noun animate
Meanings:
  • doctor
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Mu' welo'tegew, jiptug natnmi'eq malpale'wit.
    Translation: I'm not feeling well, perhaps I should go and see the doctor.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: mall路ba路lee路wit

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • malpale'wijig -- mal'pale'wijik -- doctors -- (plural)
  • malpale'wilitl -- mal'pale'wilitl -- a doctor -- (fourth person)
