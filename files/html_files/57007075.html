tapuapsgegl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Two of something globular
Part of Speech: verb inanimate intransitive
Meanings:
  • two (of something globular)
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ge'l angapte'n, pas'g tapuapsgegl alawe'l iga'tuitl.
    Translation: Look at that, he/she only put two peas there for me.
