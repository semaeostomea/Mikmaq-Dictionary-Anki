poqtigimatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she sends him/her off
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • send off
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Poqtigimateg unjann gaqatepiaqap mijipjewei ignmuaj nespnmnin.
    Translation: When he/she sent off his/her child he/she provided plenty of food for him/her to bring along.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: bohk路ti路gi路ma路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • poqtigimg -- I send him /heroff -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • poqtigimul -- I send you off -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • poqtigimit -- He/she sends me off -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
