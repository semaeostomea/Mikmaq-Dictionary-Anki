meseget

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she swallows it quickly
Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
Meanings:
  • swallow quickly
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Tetujijipaqate'lit lpa mesegei nt'pgum.
    Translation: He/she starteled me so that I swollowed my gum.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: me路se路get

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • mesegei -- I swallow it quickly -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
  • mesegeieg -- We swallow it quickly -- (first person plural exclusive animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
