neiaseniget

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: It appears shining
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • appear shining (as sun)
  • shining through (the clouds)
  • come into view with a light source
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: We'gwiawisiw neiaseniget na na'gu'set, tetujigaqi'sg winigisg'g.
    Translation: The sun seldom shines through, there's been so much inclement weather.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: ney路a路se路ni路get
