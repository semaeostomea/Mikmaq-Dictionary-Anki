giwgtoquamgwitg

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
  • Recording by rem
Translation: Water flows in a circular motion, or water flows around something
Part of Speech: verb inanimate intransitive
Meanings:
  • flows in a circular motion
  • flows around something
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Migwite'tmn ta'n eteg giwgtoquamgwitg i'lltaigup?
    Translation: Do you remember the location where the water flows in a circular motion we used to go to?
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: giw路too路hgu路am路gwitk

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • giwto'qamgwitgl -- they flow in a circle, or around something -- (third person plural inanimate)
