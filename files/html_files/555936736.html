ignmuatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she gives it to him/her
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • give to
  • allow
  • permit
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ignmuatl unjann pepsitelgenin.
    Translation: He/she allows his/her child to be disrespectful.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: i·gên·mu·a·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • ignmaq -- I give it to him/her -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • ignmul -- I give it to you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • ignmuit -- He/she gives it to me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
