menuega'tl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she needs him/her
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • need
  • need the services
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ga'qana's'g sewisga's'geg na aluse'wit menuege'g'p.
    Translation: When the doorway broke he/she needed a carpenter.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: me·nu·e·gaa·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • menuege'g -- I need him/her -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • menuego'l -- I need you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • menuege'lit -- He/she needs me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
