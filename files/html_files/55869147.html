gisa'toq

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw

  • 1.

    Translation: He/she fixes it
    Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
    Meanings:
    • fix
    • repair
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Ta'n pas'g goqwei gisa'toq negm.
      Translation: He/she can fix anything.
      Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
    Pronunciation Guide: gi·saa·dohk

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • gisa'tu -- I fix it -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
    • gisa'tueg -- We fix it -- (1p_3i)

  • 2.

    Translation: He/she is able to do it
    Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
    Meanings:
    • able to
    • can
    • capable of
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Gisa'tutesnen ntlugowaqannen..
      Translation: We will be able to do our work.
      Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
    Pronunciation Guide: gi·saa·dohk

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • gisa'tu -- I am able to do it -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
    • gisa'tueg -- We are able to do it -- (1p_3i)
