ni'pa'pit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm

Translation: He/she sits overnight at a wake or a vigil
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • sits at wake overnight
  • sits overnight at vigil
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Wela'wgul na ni'pa'pitew ugjit umijgamija.
    Translation: At night he/she will sit at wake for his/her grandfather.
