nipimatnagget

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she fights at night
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • fight at night
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Pie'l na nipimatnagget
    Translation: Peter fights at night.
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: ni路pi路ma路d锚路nak路ket

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • Nipimatnaggeieg -- We fight at night. -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • Nipimatnagga'tieg -- We fight at night. -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
