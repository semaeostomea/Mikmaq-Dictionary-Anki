waqama'taqan

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Cleanser
Part of Speech: noun inanimate
Meanings:
  • cleanser
  • washing liquid
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Waqama'taqan algwilm.
    Translation: I'm looking for the cleanser.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: wa路hga路maa路da路hgan

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • waqama'taqann -- cleansers -- (plural)
