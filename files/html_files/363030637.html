ewnasgwiet

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is dizzy
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • dizzy
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Mu getu' mnja'sigw muta ewnasgwiet menja'sij.
    Translation: He/she doesn't want to get up (from lying down) becasue he/she is dizzy when he/she gets up.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: ew·nas·kwi·et

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • ewnasgwiei -- I am dizzy -- (first person singular animate)
  • ewnasgwieieg -- We are dizzy -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • ewnaswia'tieg -- We are dizzy -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
