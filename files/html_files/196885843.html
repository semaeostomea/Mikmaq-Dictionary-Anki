gisgajiet

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she measures up to
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • have patience
  • measure up to
  • able
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Gisgajiei ugjit ms't goqwei.
    Translation: I am ready for anything.
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: gis路ka路ji路et

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • gisgajiei -- I measure up to -- (first person singular animate)
  • gisgajieieg -- We measure up to -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • gisgajia'tieg -- We measure up to -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
