esgmalatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she waits for someone
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • wait for
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Etue'l pe'l esgmalatl ugte'piteml.
    Translation: Edward, for now, waits for his wife.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: es·kê·ma·la·dêl

Related Entry: esgipa'tl
Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • esgmalg -- I wait for him/her -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • esgmalul -- I wait for you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • esgmalit -- He waits for me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
