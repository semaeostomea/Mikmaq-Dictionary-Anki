temastaqte'muet

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm

  • 1.

    Translation: He/she cuts hair
    Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
    Meanings:
    • cut hair
    • barber
    • hairdresser
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: So'sep etltemasaqte'muet a'se'g.
      Translation: Joseph is cutting hair in the other room.
      Recording: Recording by ewm
    Pronunciation Guide: de路mas-dahg-dee-mu路et

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • temastaqte'mei -- I cut hair -- (first person singular animate)
    • temastaqte'mueieg -- we cut hair -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
    • temastaqte'mua'tieg -- we cut hair -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
    Document References:
    • Pacifique Dictionary Manuscript, Page Image #787
      Transcription:temestagt猫mi, t猫m, temag, faire les cheveux (155,2) mtetjig
      Expanded:Temestagt猫mi, Temestagt猫m, Temestagtemag, faire les cheveux (155,2) Temestagt猫mtetjig
      Transliteration:Tmstaqtemai, Tmstaqtm, Tmstaqtmaq, do the hair (155,2) Tmstaqtmotejig
      Source as entry:Tmstaqtemai, I do hair
      Normalized source as entry:Tmstaqtemat, he/she cuts hair
    • Dictionary of the language of the Micmac Indians : who reside in Nova Scotia, New Brunswick, Prince Edward Island, Cape Breton and Newfoundland, Page Image #139

    • temastaqte'muenitl -- a barber, a hairdresser -- (fourth person)
