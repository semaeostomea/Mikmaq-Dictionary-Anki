mtugunoqtnugwet

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: It(horse) is behaving in a certain way when a storm is approaching
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • behave a certain way when a storm is approaching
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Mtugunoqtnugwet ntue'm, etug getu mamunigisgug sapo'nug.
    Translation: My dog is acting differently, perhaps tomorrow is going to be a stormy day.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: 锚m路tu路gu路nohk路t锚路nu路gwet
