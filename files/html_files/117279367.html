elapuguet

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she votes for
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • vote for
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: E'e, gi's gisite'tm ta'n wen elapuguei.
    Translation: Yes, I have already decided whom I will vote for.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: e路lap路bu路gu路et

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • elapuguei -- I vote for -- (first person singular animate)
  • elapugueieg -- We vote for -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • elapugua'tieg -- We vote for -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
