toqju'pilatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she hoists him/her
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • hoist
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Amujpa toqju'pilatl ugti'l muta mu gis lgusuennigul.
    Translation: He/she has to hoist his/her dog because it is incapable of the climb.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: dohk路chuu路bi路la路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • toqju'pilg -- I hoist him/her -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • toqju'pilul -- I hoist you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • toqju'pilit -- He/she hoists me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
