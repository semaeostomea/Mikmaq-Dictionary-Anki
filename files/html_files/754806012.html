neia'latl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she shows something
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • shows it
  • bring into view
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: E'pite'ji'j neia'latl ugtapsute'gann, aq teluet "pilei".
    Translation: The little girl shows her doll, and says "it's new".
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: ney路aa路la路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • neia'l'g -- I show him/her/it -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
