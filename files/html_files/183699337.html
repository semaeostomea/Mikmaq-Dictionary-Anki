telmnaqanat

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm

Translation: He/she is so weak
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • so weak
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Nitap telmnaqanat mu gismnja'sigw.
    Translation: My friend is so weak he/she can't get up.
