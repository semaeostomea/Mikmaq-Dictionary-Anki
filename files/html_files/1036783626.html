elgomigtuatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she calls out to someone
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • call out (for assistance)
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Elgomigtuatl witapal gisinisieteg.
    Translation: He/she called for assistanse from his/her buddy after his/her fall.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: el·go·mik·tu·a·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • elgomigtaq -- I call out to him for assistance -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • elgomigtul -- I call out to you for assistance -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • elgomigtuit -- He/she calls out to me for assistance -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
