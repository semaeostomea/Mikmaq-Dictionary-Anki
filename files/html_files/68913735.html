telistaqanewa'sit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she hears things that are not there
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • hears things that are not there
  • thinks one hears something
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Pemjipaqa'sit aq mu gne'nug natgoqwei piltuei telistaqanewa'sit.
    Translation: He/she is becoming fearful and every so often imagines that he/she hears something different.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: de·lis·da·hga·ne·waa·sit

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • telistaqanewa'si -- I hear things -- (first person singular animate)
  • telistaqanewa'tieg -- We hear things -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • telistaqanewita'ieg -- We hear things -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
