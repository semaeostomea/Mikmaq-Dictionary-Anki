pugwelijuit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she flows extensively
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • flow extensively
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ntsamqwano'qm pugwelijuit.
    Translation: My water bottle flows extensively.
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: bu·gwe·li·ju·it

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • pugwelijuijig -- they/those flow extensively -- (third person dual animate)
  • pugwelijuultijig -- they/those flow extensively -- (third person plural animate)
