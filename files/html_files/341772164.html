pangeji'j

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Pung
Part of Speech: noun inanimate
Meanings:
  • pung
  • horse drawn winter sleigh on runners
  • sleigh
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Mu na nugu' wen nemiamg alteja'lugwet pangeji'jigtug.
    Translation: One no longer sees anyone riding around in a pung.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: ban路ge路jiich

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • pangeji'tl -- pungs -- (plural)
