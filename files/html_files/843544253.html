nantuat

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she hunts eels in mud
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • hunt eels in mud
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Mu nugu' ni'n migwite'lmaq wen nantuan.
    Translation: I can't remember anyone who hunts for eels in the mud in winter.
