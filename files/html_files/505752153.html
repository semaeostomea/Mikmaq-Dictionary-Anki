elatejitoq

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she drags it over
Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
Meanings:
  • drag over
  • drag toward
  • pull to
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Elatejitoq tepaqanji'j ta'n etlmila'sij.
    Translation: He/she drags the toy truck/car to where he/she is playing.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: e·la·de·ji·dohk

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • elatejitu -- I drag it over -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
  • elatejitueg -- We drag it over -- (first person plural exclusive animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
