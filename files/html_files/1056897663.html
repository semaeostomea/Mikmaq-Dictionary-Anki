mawisetesing

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm

Translation: He/she contracts
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • contract
  • cringe
  • fall in heap
  • crumple from a fall
  • shrink from
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Go'gwejij lpa mawisetesintew te's samqwan elapaqto'j.
    Translation: A spider will actually cringe whenever water is splashed at him/her.
