tetaqulguni

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Kingbird
Part of Speech: noun animate
Meanings:
  • kingbird
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Tetaqulguni na sisip awisiw etlnmu't ula gm'tginaq.
    Translation: The Kingbird is not often seen here in our territory.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: de路da路hgul路ku路ni

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • tetaqulguni'aq -- kingbirds -- (plural)
  • tetaqulgunial -- a kingbird -- (fourth person)
