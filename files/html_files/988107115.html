jilapa'latl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she makes scratch marks on him/her
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • make scratch marks
  • scratches
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Wen gis(i)jilapa'latl ntue'ml?
    Translation: Who has scratched my pet?
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: ji·la·baa·la·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • jilapa'l'g -- I make a scratch mark on him/her -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • jilapa'lul -- I make a scratch mark on you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • jilapa'lit -- He/she makes a scratch mark on me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
