jipaqa'sit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she gets scared
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • get scared
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Jijuaqa na wen jipaqa'sit.
    Translation: Sometimes one gets sacred.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: ji·ba·hgaa·sit

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • jipaqa'tieg -- we are getting scared -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • jipaqitaieg -- we are getting scared -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
