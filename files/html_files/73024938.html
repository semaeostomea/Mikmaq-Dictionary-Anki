nenaq'ta'sit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she thinks quickly
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • thinks quickly
  • brain racing
  • anxious
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Nenaq'ta'sit, sapo'nug maja'sit.
    Translation: He/she is anxious, he/she leaves tomorrow.
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: ne路na路hg锚路taa路sit

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • nenaq'ta'si -- I think quickly, I am anxious, my brain is racing -- (first person singular animate)
  • nenaq'ta'sieg -- We think quickly, we are anxious, our brains are racing -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • nenaq'ta'sultieg -- We think quickly, we are anxious, our brains are racing -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
