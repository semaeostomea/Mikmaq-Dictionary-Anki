jimpegnewet

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw

  • 1.

    Translation: He/she milks
    Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
    Meanings:
    • milk (cow)
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: So'sep jimpegnewet egsitpu'g.
      Translation: Joseph milks in the morning.
      Recording: Recording by ewm
    Pronunciation Guide: jim路pe路g锚路ne路wet

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • jimpegnewei -- I milk -- (first person singular animate)
    • jimpegneweieg -- We milk -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
    • jimpegnewa'utieg -- We milk -- (first person plural exclusive animate)

  • 2.

    Translation: He/she wrings out
    Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
    Meanings:
    • wring out
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Etljimpegnewet pugwelg wesgo'tg.
      Translation: The one doing the wringing has alot to do.
      Recording: Recording by dmm
