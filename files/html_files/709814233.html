niwiga's'g

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she wipes it dry
Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
Meanings:
  • wipe dry
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ge' niwiga'se'n pata'uti?
    Translation: Could you wipe the table dry?
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: ni·wi·gaa·sêk

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • niwiga'smann -- I wipe it dry -- (first person singular animate subject, third person plural inanimate object)
  • niwiga'smegl -- We wipe it dry -- (1p-3ip)
