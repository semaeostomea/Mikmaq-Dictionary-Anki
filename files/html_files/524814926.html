oqonisgwa'latl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she covers him/her
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • cover
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Sangew oqonisgwa'latl mijua'ji'tl a'su'nigtug, wejo'tg mu tugwa'lan.
    Translation: He/she gently covers the baby in a blanket, trying not to awaken him/her.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: o路hgo路nis路kwaa路la路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • oqonisgwa'l'g -- I cover him/her -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • oqonisgwa'lul -- I cover you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • oqonisgwa'lit -- He/she covers me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
