alje'muinu

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Baseball player
Part of Speech: noun animate
Meanings:
  • baseball player
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Taluisit ala alje'muinu?
    Translation: What is that baseball player's name?
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: al·chee·mu·i·nu

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • alje'muinu'g -- baseball players -- (plural)
  • alje'muinu'l -- a baseball player -- (fourth person)
