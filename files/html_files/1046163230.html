ewnasiet

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is confused
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • confused
  • gone awry
  • mixed up
  • topsy turvy
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Nmu'ji'j ewnasiet, uggwitl e'natl.
    Translation: The puppy is confused, he/she has lost his/her mom.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: ew路na路si路et

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • ewnasiei -- I am confused -- ()
  • ewnasieieg -- We are confused -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • ewnasia'tieg -- We are confused -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
