wesua'teget

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she hauls in a pot (in a poker game)
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • haul in pot (in poker game)
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Etug i't'tew na'gweg wesua'tegei?
    Translation: Will there ever be a day that I haul in the poker pot?
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: we·su·aa·de·get

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • wesua'tegei -- I haul in a pot (in a poker game) -- (first person singular animate)
  • wesua'tegeieg -- We haul in a pot (in a poker game) -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • wesua'tega'tieg -- We haul in a pot (in a poker game) -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
