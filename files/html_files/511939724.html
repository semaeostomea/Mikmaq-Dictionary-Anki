uggamlamun

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw

  • 1.

    Translation: His/her heart
    Part of Speech: noun animate
    Meanings:
    • heart
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Teluet uggamlamun sewisge'g.
      Translation: He/she says his/her heart is broken.
      Recording: Recording by jnw
    Pronunciation Guide: uk路kam路la路mun

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • ngamlamun, ntgamlamun -- my heart -- (1(3))
    • ngamlamuninal -- our(x) hearts -- (1x(3p))
    • uggamlamunual -- their hearts -- (3p(4p))

  • 2.

    Translation: Heart (in playing cards)
    Part of Speech: noun animate
    Meanings:
    • heart (in cards)
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Ta'puewa'j uggamlamun geggunt?
      Translation: Do you have the two of hearts?
      Recording: Recording by dmm
    Pronunciation Guide: uk路kam路la路mun

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • uggamlamung -- hearts -- (plural)
    • uggamlamunl -- a heart -- (fourth person)
