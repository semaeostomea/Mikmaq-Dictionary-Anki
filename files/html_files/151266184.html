se'sa'toq

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw

  • 1.

    Translation: He/she messes it up
    Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
    Meanings:
    • mess up
    • disarray
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: So'sep na gis se'sa'toq unpo'gon.
      Translation: Joe the one who messed up his/her own bed.
      Recording: Recording by ewm
    Pronunciation Guide: see路saa路dohk

  • 2.

    Translation: He/she spreads it
    Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
    Meanings:
    • spread
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: T'pgwan se'so'tun, mutt wanta'siw lo'tun ala gmetug.
      Translation: When you spread the soil, don't forget to put some on that side.
      Recording: Recording by dmm
    Pronunciation Guide: see路saa路dohk

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • se'sa'tu -- I spread it -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
    • se'sa'tueg -- We spread it -- (first person plural exclusive animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
