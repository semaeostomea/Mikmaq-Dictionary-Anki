tmigalaw

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Rib of a ship
Part of Speech: noun inanimate
Meanings:
  • rib of ship
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Newtei tmigalaw mu assma weltestnug.
    Translation: One of the ship ribs doesn't fit properly.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: dêm·i·kal·aw

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • tmigalawl -- ribs -- (plural)
