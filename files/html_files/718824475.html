aptlama'toq

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she smothers it
Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
Meanings:
  • smother (fire)
  • block off air from
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Epsaqtejgl gepsoqta'teg, na aptlama'toq pugtew.
    Translation: When he/she closed the draft on the stove, it smothered the fire.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: ap路t锚路la路maa路dohk

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • aptlama'tu -- aptlama'tu -- I smother it -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
  • aptlama'tueg -- aptlama'tuek -- We smother it -- (first person plural exclusive animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
