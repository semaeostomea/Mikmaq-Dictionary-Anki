ilpilatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw

  • 1.

    Translation: He/she provides clothing for him/her/it
    Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
    Meanings:
    • provide clothing for
    • dress up
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Mali ilpilatl ugtapsute'gann.
      Translation: Mary dresses up her doll.
      Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
    Pronunciation Guide: il·pi·la·dêl

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • ilpilg -- I provide clothing for him/her/it -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
    • ilpilul -- I provides clothing for you -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
    • ilpilit -- He/she provides clothing for me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)

  • 2.

    Translation: He/she gets it ready for shipping
    Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
    Meanings:
    • get ready for shipping
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Amqwanji'jg ilpilgig ugjit pqotgiman.
      Translation: I am wrapping spoons for shipping.
      Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
    Pronunciation Guide: il·pi·la·dêl

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • ilpilg -- I get it ready for shipping -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
    • ilpilgig -- I get them ready for shipping -- (first person singular animate subject, third person plural animate object)
