gesgoqomaqasit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is broad beamed
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • broad beamed
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Gesgoqomaqasit na ala enmejepuguit.
    Translation: The one standing with his/her back towards us is broad beamed.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: ges路ko路hgo路ma路hga路sit

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • gesgoqomaqasi -- I am broad beamed -- (first person singular animate)
  • gesgoqomaqasieg -- We are broad beamed -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • gesgoqomaqasultieg -- We are broad beamed -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
