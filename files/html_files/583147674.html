sasqapsgeg

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Plateau/table rock
Part of Speech: verb inanimate intransitive
Meanings:
  • plateau
  • table rock
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Gisgug pas'g gwilmu'gl sasqapgegl.
    Translation: Today we will only look for flat round rocks.
    Recording: Recording by ewm
