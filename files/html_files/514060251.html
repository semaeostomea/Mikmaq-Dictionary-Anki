usgagelsuti

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Greeting
Part of Speech: noun inanimate
Meanings:
  • greeting
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Pewatmu'g oqwaita'tij nimitunew mesgi'g usgagelsuti.
    Translation: We want them to see a big greeting when they arrive.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: us路ka路gel路su路di

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
