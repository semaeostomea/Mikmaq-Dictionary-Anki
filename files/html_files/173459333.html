tewatejitoq

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she drags it out
Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
Meanings:
  • drag out
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Tewatejitoq sa'qawei utepaqan.
    Translation: He/she drags it out his/hers old car.
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: de路wa路de路ji路dohk

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • tewatejitu -- I drag it out -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
  • tewatejitueg -- We drag it out -- (first person plural exclusive animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
