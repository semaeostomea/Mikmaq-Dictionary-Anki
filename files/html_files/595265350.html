gisgaja'latl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she gets it(turkey) ready
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • get ready
  • prepare
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Gisgaja'latl ap'tapegiejinitl glamen giswissugwalatal.
    Translation: He/she is preparing the turkey so he/she can cook it.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: gis·ka·jaa·la·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • gisgaja'l'g -- I get it ready -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
