sa'se'wa'latl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she exchanges him/her
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • exchange
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Getu' sa'se'wa'latl gispisunn pas'g mu we'jiagul.
    Translation: He/she would like to exchange the belt but can't find it.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: saa路see路waa路la路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • sa'se'wa'l'g -- I exchange him/her -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
