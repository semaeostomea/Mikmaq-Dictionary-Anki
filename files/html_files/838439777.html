me'teluatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she suceeds at shooting him/her/it
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • succeed at shooting
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Gaqi'sg pe'sguatl, pas'g newt me'teluatl.
    Translation: He/she shot him/her often, but he/she only hit him/her once.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: mee路de路lu路a路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • me'telaq -- I succeed at shooting him/her/it -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • me'telul -- I succeed at shooting you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • me'teluit -- He/she succeeds at shooting me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
