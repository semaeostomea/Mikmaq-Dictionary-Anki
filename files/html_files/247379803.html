emegwatalg

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she eats disgusting food
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • eat disgusting food
  • eat in a disgusting manner
  • messy eater
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Emegwatalg na ula gigmenu.
    Translation: Our buddy a messy eater.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: e路me路gwa路dalk

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • emegwatal -- I eat disgusting food -- (first person singular animate)
  • emegwataleg -- We eat disgusting food -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • emegwatalultieg -- We eat disgusting food -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
