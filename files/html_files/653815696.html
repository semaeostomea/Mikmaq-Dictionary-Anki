memgoqte'get

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is making a clearing
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • make a clearing
  • deforest
  • clear cutting
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Egsitpu'nug so'qiet aq poqjimemgoqte'get.
