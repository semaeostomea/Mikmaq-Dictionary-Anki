megwatetesing

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: It(bulb) flashes red
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • flash red
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Newte'git gloqowej nemi'g lpa tluenes megwatetesing.
    Translation: I see one star that you could say is flashing red.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: me·gwa·de·de·sink
