lu'suesgwewa'sit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: She performs the duties of a prospective daughter-in-law
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • perform duties of prospective daughter·in·law
  • become a prospective daughter·in·law
  • become a daughter·in·law
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Pugjig lu'suesgwewa'sit ula gitape'sgwinu.
    Translation: Soon our female buddy will become a daughter-in-law.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: luu·su·es·kwe·waa·sit

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • lu'suesgwewa'si -- I become or perform duties of prospective daughter-in-law -- (first person singular animate)
  • lu'suesgwewa'tieg -- We become or perform duties of prospective daughter-in-law -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • lu'suesgwewita'ieg -- We become or perform duties of prospective daughter-in-law -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
