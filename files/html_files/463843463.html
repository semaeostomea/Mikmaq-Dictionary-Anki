esp'saqteg

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: It is high
Part of Speech: verb inanimate intransitive
Meanings:
  • high
  • high (sharp) angle
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Eimu'tijig te'pljug gistesgmi'tij esp'saqteg.
    Translation: There are goats able to make their way on high steep terrain.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: es·pês·ahk·teg

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • esp'saqtegl -- those are high -- (third person plural inanimate)
Document References:
  • Pacifique Dictionary Manuscript, Page Image #172
    Transcription:espesagteg, c’est élevé
    Transliteration:espesaqteg, it is high
    Source as entry:espesagteg, it is high
    Normalized source as entry:esp'saqteg, it is high
  • Dictionary of the language of the Micmac Indians : who reside in Nova Scotia, New Brunswick, Prince Edward Island, Cape Breton and Newfoundland, Page Image #144
    Public Note:ĕspāsâktĕk', espe’saqteg
