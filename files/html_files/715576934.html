ewlapewit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she looks sad
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • have a sad expression
  • look sad
  • look wan
  • look sickly
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ula nitape'sgw ewlapewit.
    Translation: My friend (female) looks sad.
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: ew路la路be路wit

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • ewlapewi -- I look sad -- (first person singular animate)
  • ewlapewieg -- We look sad -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • ewlapewultieg -- We look sad -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
