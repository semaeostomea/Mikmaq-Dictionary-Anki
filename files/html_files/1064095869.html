teliganig

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: It is built in that manner
Part of Speech: verb inanimate intransitive
Meanings:
  • built in that manner
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ge' neia'tui ta'n tliganitew gi'g?
    Translation: Can you please show me how your house will be built?
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: de路li路ka路nig

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • teliganigl -- they are built in that manner -- (third person plural inanimate)
