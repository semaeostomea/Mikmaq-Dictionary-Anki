wetnasa'latl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she tries...on
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • try on
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Wetnasa'latl pile'l ugtatlaiml.
    Translation: He/she tries on his/her new shirt.
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: we·dê·na·saa·la·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • wetnasa'l'g -- I try...on -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
