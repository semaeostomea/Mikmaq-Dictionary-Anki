napgwetoq

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she copies it
Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
Meanings:
  • copy
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Telintawisgnuet na negm, pas'g goqwei angapt'g na gisnapgwetoq.
    Translation: He/she is such a good knitter that all he/she has to do is look at something and he/she can make a copy of it.
