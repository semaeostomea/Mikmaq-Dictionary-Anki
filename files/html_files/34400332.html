gepjoqi'satl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she sews him/her/it up
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • sew up
  • sew shut
  • sew closed
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Gepjoqi'satl ugtatla'iml.
    Translation: He sews his shirt up./She sews her shirt up.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: gep·cho·hgii·sa·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • gepjoqi's'g -- I sew him/her up -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
