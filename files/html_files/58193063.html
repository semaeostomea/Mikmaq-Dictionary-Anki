gesga'sit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw

  • 1.

    Translation: He/she disappears
    Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
    Meanings:
    • disappear
    • become invisible
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Tami etlgesga'sitaq na wi'sisag?
      Translation: Where did that animal disappear to?
      Recording: Recording by ewm
    Pronunciation Guide: ges路kaa路sit

  • 2.

    Translation: It becomes wider
    Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
    Meanings:
    • wide
    • become wider
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Ta'n tujiw saqpa'l'j na telamugsit a'papi, na gesga'sit.
      Translation: When you wet that type of rope, it widens.
      Recording: Recording by jnw
    Pronunciation Guide: ges路kaa路sit
