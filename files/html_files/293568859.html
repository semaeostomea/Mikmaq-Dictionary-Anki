temisgunatoq

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she cuts/breaks off the bud or sprout
Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
Meanings:
  • cut the bud or sprout
  • break off bud or sprout
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Temisguna'toql weljema'jgewe'l.
    Translation: He/she breaks off the sweetgrass sprouts.
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: de路mis路gu路naa路dohk

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • temisguna'tu -- I cut/break off the bud or sprout -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
  • temisguna'tueg -- we cut/break off the bud or sprout -- (first person plural exclusive animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
Document References:
  • Pacifique Dictionary Manuscript, Page Image #787
    Transcription:temisgonato, couper les boutons, les germes.
    Transliteration:temisqunatu, cut the buds, sprouts.
    Source as entry:temisqunatu, I cut the bud, sprout
    Normalized source as entry:temisqunatoq, he/she cuts the bud or sprout
  • Dictionary of the language of the Micmac Indians : who reside in Nova Scotia, New Brunswick, Prince Edward Island, Cape Breton and Newfoundland, Page Image #261
