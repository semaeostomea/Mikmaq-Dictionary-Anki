menuegetoq

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she needs it
Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
Meanings:
  • need
  • has use for
  • could use
  • require
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Menuegetueg Mi'gmewei tli'suti.
    Translation: We need the Mi'gmaq language.
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: me路nu路e路ge路dohk

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • menuegetu -- I need it -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
  • menuegetueg -- We need it -- (first person plural exclusive animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
