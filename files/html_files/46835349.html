wajua'toq

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she fills it up with non·liquid
Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
Meanings:
  • fill up (with non·liquid)
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Geget wajua'toq utepaqan.
    Translation: He/she has almost filled his/her car.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: wa·ju·aa·dohk

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • wajua'tu -- I fill it up with non·liquid -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
  • wajua'tueg -- We fill it up with non·liquid -- (first person plural exclusive animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
