ejigligsma'latl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she pushes him/her away
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • push away
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ejigligsma'latl uggwe'ji'tl.
    Translation: He/she pushes his/her younger sister away.
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: e路jig路lik路s锚路maa路la路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • ejigligsma'l'g -- I push him away -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • ejigligsma'lul -- I push you away -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • ejigligsma'lit -- He/she pushes me away -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
