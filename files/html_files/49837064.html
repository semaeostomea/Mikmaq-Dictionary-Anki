a'sutmat

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she prays
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • pray
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Gelu'lg wen egsitpu'gl a'sutmaj.
    Translation: It's good to pray in the mornings.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: aa路su路d锚路mat

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • a'sutmai -- I pray -- (first person singular animate)
  • a'sutmaieg -- We pray -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • a'sutma'tieg -- We pray -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
