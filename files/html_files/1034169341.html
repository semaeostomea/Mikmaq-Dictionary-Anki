usaqan

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Shoulder blade
Part of Speech: noun animate
Meanings:
  • shoulder blade
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Gigji'w usaqanigtug wejiaq wius mawwigapu'g.
    Translation: Meat from close to the shoulder blade is very delicious.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
