me'sipmiet

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is incapable of walking
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • incapable of walking
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ugjigunn tewjigsnugwatl lpa me'sipmiet.
    Translation: His/her knee is so sore he/she can not walk.
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: mee·si·bê·mi·et

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • me'sipmiei -- I am incapable of walking -- (first person singular animate)
  • me'sipma'tieg -- We are incapable of walking -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • me'sipmita'ieg -- We are incapable of walking -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
