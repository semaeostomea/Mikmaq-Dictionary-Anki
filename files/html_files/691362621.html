wasoqonatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she keeps him/her/it illuminated
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • illuminate
  • keep light on
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Sosep wasoqonatl ugta'piml ge's etla'pigej.
    Translation: Joseph keeps a light on his net while he's fishing.
    Recording: Recording by jnw

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • wasoqong -- I keep him/her/it illuminated -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
