espaqamigeg

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Hill
Part of Speech: noun inanimate
Meanings:
  • hill
  • small hill
  • high land
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Espaqamigeg pilei nignen ta'n eteg.
    Translation: The hill is high where we my new house is situated.
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: es·pa·hka·mi·kek

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • espaqamigegl -- hills -- (plural)
