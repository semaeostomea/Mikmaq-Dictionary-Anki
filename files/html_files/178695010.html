sewtaqa'taqan

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Barge
Part of Speech: noun inanimate
Meanings:
  • barge
  • raft
  • trailer
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ta'sisgegipunqeg etug ta'n sewtaqa'taqan giaspnmitas'g ula Listugujg.
    Translation: How many years has it been since a barge was seen here in Listuguj.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: sew路ta路hgaa路da路hgan

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • sewtaqa'taqann -- barges -- (plural)
