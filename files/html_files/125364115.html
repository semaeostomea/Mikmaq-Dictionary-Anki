anguna'latl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she covers him
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • cover
  • encase
  • enclose
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Mu welamagul na anguna'latl.
    Translation: He/she finds it (animate) unattractive, so he/she covers it (animate).
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: an·ku·naa·la·dêl

Related Entry: anguna'toq
Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • anguana'l'g -- ankuana'l'k -- I cover him -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • anguna'lul -- ankuna'lul -- I cover you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • anguna'lit -- ankuna'lit -- He/she covers me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
