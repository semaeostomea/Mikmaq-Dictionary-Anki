gjitmei

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw

  • 1.

    Translation: He/she/it is the genuine one
    Part of Speech: verb inanimate intransitive
    Meanings:
    • genuine
    • real one
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Gjitmei elege'wi'sgw taluisit?
      Translation: What is the real queen's name?
      Recording: Recording by dmm
    Pronunciation Guide: 锚k路chi路d锚路mey

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • gjitme'l -- they are genuine ones -- (third person plural inanimate)

  • 2.

    Translation: It is the genuine one
    Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
    Meanings:
    • genuine
    • real one
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Tapusijig lapolg epitjig, pas'g newte'jit gjitmei.
      Translation: There are two bowls there, but only one is real.
      Recording: Recording by jnw
    Pronunciation Guide: 锚k路chi路d锚路mey

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • gjitme'g -- they are genuine ones -- (third person plural animate)

  • 3.

    Translation: Really
    Part of Speech: particle
    Meanings:
    • really
    • actually
    • truly
    • factually
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Gjitmei teliaqas na?
      Translation: Did that actually happen?
      Recording: Recording by dmm
    Pronunciation Guide: 锚k路chi路d锚路mey
