tmgwatignej

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Heron
Part of Speech: noun animate
Meanings:
  • heron
  • crane
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Enm'papga'timg jijuaqa nemu't tmgwatignej etliwsget gigjiw sitmug.
    Translation: Going down the coast sometimes one can see a heron fishing for food by the shore.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
