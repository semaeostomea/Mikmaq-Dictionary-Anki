toqju'pilawet

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she hoists
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • hoist
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Sa'n etltoqju'pilawet, negm pipanim.
    Translation: Sean is hoisting things, ask him.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: dohk路chuu路bi路la路wet

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • toqju'pilawei -- I hoist -- (first person singular animate)
  • toqju'pilaweieg -- we hoist -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • toqju'pilawa'tieg, toqju'pilawa'utieg -- we hoist -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
