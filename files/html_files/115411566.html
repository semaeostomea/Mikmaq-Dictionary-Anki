miti'ji'j

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Sapling
Part of Speech: noun animate
Meanings:
  • sapling
  • small tree
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Pugwelgig miti'ji'jg gisiga'taqalgig.
    Translation: I planted many saplings.
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: mi路dii路chiich

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • miti'ji'jg -- saplings -- (plural)
  • miti'ji'jl, miti'ji'tl -- a sapling -- (fourth person)
