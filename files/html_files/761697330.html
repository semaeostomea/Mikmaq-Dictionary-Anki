nipetluat

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she monitors for or follows (beaver, moose, other animals) at night
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • monitors for (beaver, moose, other animals) at night
  • watches for (beaver, moose, other animals) at night
  • scouts for (beaver, moose, other animals) at night
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Pie'l na nipetluatew te'siula'gw.
    Translation: Peter will scout for animals each night.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: ni路be路d锚l路u路at

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • nipetluaieg -- we monitor/watch/scout for/follow animals at night -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • nipetlua'tieg -- we monitor/watch/scout for/follow animals at night -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
