malja't

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she staggers slowly
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • stagger slowly
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ala' ji'nm malja't, etug tala'teget?
    Translation: That staggering man overthere, what do you think is wrong with him?
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: mal路chaad

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • malja'i -- I stagger slowly -- (first person singular animate)
  • malja'ieg -- We stagger slowly -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • maljo'ltieg -- We stagger slowly -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
