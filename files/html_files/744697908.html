maltewsatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she causes him/her/it to bleed by cutting
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • cause to bleed by cutting
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Nujitmasaqte'muet gis maltewsatl ji'nmul ge's etlpesgutua'j.
    Translation: The barber made the man bleed while he/she was shaving him.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: mall·dew·sa·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • maltews'g -- mal'tews'k -- I cause him/her/it to bleed by cutting -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • maltewsul -- mal'tewsul -- I cause you to bleed by cutting -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • maltewsit -- mal'tewsit -- He/she causes me to bleed by cutting -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
