welinqana'mat

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she has a good time drinking
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • has a good time drinking
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Welinqana'ma'tie'g wela'gwe'g gatu gesnugutieg nige' ula egsitpu'g.
    Translation: We all had a good time drinking last night but we are all sick this morning.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: we路lin路hka路naa路mat

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • welinqana'mai -- I have a good time drinking -- (first person singular animate)
  • welinqana'maieg -- We have a good time drinking -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • welinqana'ma'tieg -- We have a good time drinking -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
