esna'toq

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm

Translation: He/she/it packs it down
Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
Meanings:
  • pack down
  • press down
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Uliesna'tu ms't goqwei, me' nqamse'gt'tew ta'n telapjimg.
    Translation: Pack down everything well, it will be easier to handle.
