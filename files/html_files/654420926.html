pesa'latl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she skins him/her (fish)
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • skin fish
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Pe'talaji, waqama'laji, toqo tujiw pesa'laji.
    Translation: He/she catches them (fish), guts them, then skins them.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: be路saa路la路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • pesa'l'g -- I skin him/her (fish) -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
