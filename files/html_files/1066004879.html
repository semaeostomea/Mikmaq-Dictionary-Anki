waqjuiga's'g

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: It becomes crooked
Part of Speech: verb inanimate intransitive
Meanings:
  • become crooked
  • droop
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Waqjuiga's'g pgisinn, na inaqaneg eteg.
    Translation: When you get to the bend, it is on the right.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
