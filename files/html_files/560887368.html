Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm

Translation: He/she stings it
Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
Meanings:
  • sting
  • pierce
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Amu gis sapte'lit npaqamg.
    Translation: A bee just stung my back.
