negmewei

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw

  • 1.

    Translation: His/hers/its
    Part of Speech: noun animate
    Meanings:
    • his
    • hers
    • its
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Negmewei na a'gwesn?
      Translation: Its that his/her hat?
      Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
    Pronunciation Guide: ne·gê·me·wey

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • negmewe'g -- They are his/hers -- (plural)

  • 2.

    Translation: His/hers/its
    Part of Speech: noun inanimate
    Meanings:
    • his
    • hers
    • its
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Telta'siap negla negmewe'l.
      Translation: I thought that those were his/hers/its.
      Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
    Pronunciation Guide: ne·gê·me·wey

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • negmewe'l -- They are his/hers -- (plural)
