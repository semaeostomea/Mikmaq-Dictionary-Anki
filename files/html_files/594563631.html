sa'se'wa'tasit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she/it is changed
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • is changed
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Pile'g ula napui'gigng, ta'n sa'se'wa'tasisnig?
    Translation: These are new photographs, when were they changed?
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: saa路see路waa路da路sit

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • sa'se'wa'l'g -- I change him/her -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • sa'se'wa'lul -- I change you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • sa'se'wa'lit -- he/she/it changed me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
Document References:
  • Pacifique Dictionary Manuscript, Page Image #735
    Transcription:saseoatasi, changer d'id茅e, 锚tre chang茅 (Pi liet. saseoit)
    Transliteration:sa'se'wa'tasi, change your mind, become changed (Pi liet. sa'se'wit)
    Source as entry:sa'se'wa'tasi, I change my ideas, I am changed
    Normalized source as entry:sa'se'wa'tasit, he/she/it is changed (ideas)
  • Rand's Micmac dictionary from phonographic word-lists - transcribed and alphabetically arranged, with a grammar and list of place-names by Jeremiah S. Clark, Page Image #184
