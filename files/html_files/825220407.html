mesaqaneita'tl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she knocks him/her down
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • knock down
  • collaspes as a result of a punch
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Matnaggewinu mesaqaneita'tl igtigl matnaggewinu'l.
    Translation: The fighter flattens the other fighter.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: me路sahk路a路ney路daa路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • mesaqaneite'g -- I knock him/her down -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • mesaqaneito'l -- I knock you down -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • mesaqaneite'lit -- He/she knocks me down -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
