sasqale's

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm

Translation: Scallop
Part of Speech: noun animate
Meanings:
  • scallop
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Tami etligtanujig sasqale'sg?
    Translation: Where do you fish for scallops?
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: sas路hka路lees

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • sasqale'sg, sasqale'sig -- scallops -- (plural)
  • sasqale'sl -- a scallop -- (fourth person)
