u'ta'qan

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Paddle
Part of Speech: noun inanimate
Meanings:
  • paddle
  • oar
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: U'ta'qann tapu'gul wegetesg.
    Translation: I will need two paddles.
    Recording: Recording by dmm

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
