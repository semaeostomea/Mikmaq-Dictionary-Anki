mawisgnuatg

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she knits it together
Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
Meanings:
  • knit together
  • braid together
  • weave together
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Mawisgnuatgl newgl pilue'l jijgluewjuapi'l.
    Translation: He/she knits four kinds of wool together.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
