tetapueiwatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she treats him/her correctly/properly
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • treat properly
  • take care of properly
  • look after properly
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Pie'l na mawtetapueiwatl ugtue'ml.
    Translation: Peter treats his animal properly.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: de路da路bu路ey路wa路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • tetapueiaq -- I treat him/her properly -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • tetapueiul -- I treat you properly -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • tetapueiwit -- he/she treats me properly -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
