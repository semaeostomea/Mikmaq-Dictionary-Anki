pesqatesmatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she scrapes it(knee)
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • scrape
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Gisipsoqtesgmat aq pesqatesmatl ugjigun.
    Translation: S\he just tripped and s\he scraped his\her knee.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: bes路hka路de路s锚路ma路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
