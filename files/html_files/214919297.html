pitgmalatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she fills up him/her/it
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • fill up
  • stuff
  • load
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Sangew pitgmalatl ugsamqwano'qgml.
    Translation: He/she slowly fills his/her water jug.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: bit路k锚路ma路la路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • pitgmalg -- I fill up him/her/it -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • pitgmalul -- I fill you up -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • pitgmalit -- He/she fills me up -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
