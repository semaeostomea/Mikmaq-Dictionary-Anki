pisgweto't

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw

  • 1.

    Translation: He/she/it is elected
    Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
    Meanings:
    • elected
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Ne's'sijig pile'smug pisgweto'jig.
      Translation: Three new candidates were elected.
      Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
    Pronunciation Guide: bis·kwe·dood

  • 2.

    Translation: He/she/it hits it into (net, goal or hole)
    Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
    Meanings:
    • hit into (net, goal or hole)
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Lpa pisgweta'tl tu'aqann unji e'w'g.
      Translation: He/she knocked the ball in using his/her head.
      Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
    Pronunciation Guide: bis·kwe·dood

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • pisgwete'g -- I hit him/her/it in -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
    • pisgweto'l -- I hit you in -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
    • pisgwete'lit -- He/she hits me in -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
