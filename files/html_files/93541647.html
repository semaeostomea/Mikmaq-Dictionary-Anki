mejigamugsit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she has a dirty appearance
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • have dirty appearance
  • look dirty
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Sisgu gisiamjaqusiteg, na toqo tujiw mejegamugsit Wapigut.
    Translation: After she had smeered herself in the mud, that's when Wapigut (White-Pawed) looked really dirty.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: me·ji·ga·muk·sit

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • mejigamugsi -- I have a dirty appearance -- (first person singular animate)
  • mejigamugsieg -- We have a dirty appearance -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • mejigamugsultieg -- We have a dirty appearance -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
