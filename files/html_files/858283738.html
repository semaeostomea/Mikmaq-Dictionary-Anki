jipaqa'latl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she frightens him
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • frighten
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ula nmu'j gisjipaqa'latl n'njann.
    Translation: This dog has scared my child.
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: ji路ba路hgaa路la路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • jipaqa'l'g -- I frighten him -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • jipaqa'lul -- I frighten you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • jipaqa'lit -- He frightens me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
