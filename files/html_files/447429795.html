na'ntemigjematl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she checks him/her/it for fleas
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • check him/her/it for fleas
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Na'ntemigjematl ge's etlatalnij.
    Translation: He/she checks him/her for fleas while he/she is eating.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: naan·dêm·ig·che·ma·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • na'ntemigjemg -- I check him/her/it for fleas -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • na'ntemigjemul -- I check you for fleas -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • na'ntemigjemit -- he/she checks me for fleas -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
