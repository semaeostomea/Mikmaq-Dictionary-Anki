wejguigaqawapuguet

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she comes scolding or reprimanding
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • come scolding
  • come reprimanding
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Gi's ap ula gigmenu wejguigaqawapuguet.
    Translation: Here comes our kin scolding again.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: wech·kwi·ga·hga·wap·bu·gu·et

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • wejguigaqawapuguei -- I come scolding or reprimanding -- (first person singular animate)
  • wejguigaqawapugueieg -- We come scolding or reprimanding -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • wejguigaqawapugua'tieg -- We come scolding or reprimanding -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
