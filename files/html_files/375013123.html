natawa'qa'toq

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is competent in doing or handling it
Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
Meanings:
  • skillful in handling
  • competent in doing
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ne'gaw na tele'g, natawa'qa'toq ta'n getu' goqwei.
    Translation: He/she has always been that way, skillful at everything and anything.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: na路da路waa路hgaa路dohk

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • natawa'qa'tu -- I am competent in doing it -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
  • natawa'qa'tueg -- We are competent in doing it -- (first person plural exclusive animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
