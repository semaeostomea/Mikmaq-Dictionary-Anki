enmuggwalatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she follows someone home or onward
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • follow onward
  • follow home
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Te'sigisg'g ala lmu'j enmuggwalatl na lpa'tujl.
    Translation: Every day that dog follows the boy home.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: en路muk路kwa路la路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • enmuggwalg -- I follow him home -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • enmuggwalul -- I follow you home -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • enmuggwalit -- He follows me home -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
