nujgmoqsatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she melts it (by using heat)
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • soften by using heat
  • melt by using heat
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Wen gisinujgmoqsatl?
    Translation: Who melted it?
    Recording: Recording by ewm
