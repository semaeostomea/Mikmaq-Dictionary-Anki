qomuti

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Harbour, haven
Part of Speech: noun inanimate
Meanings:
  • harbour
  • haven
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ula mujga'wane'g Listugujg amgwes i'qomutit'p.
    Translation: Here at the far end of Listuguj cove used to be a safe haven.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: hgo路mu路di

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • qomuti'l -- harbours -- (plural)
