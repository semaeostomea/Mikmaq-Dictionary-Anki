nsanoqonig

Recordings:

Translation: It is dangerous
Part of Speech: verb inanimate intransitive
Meanings:
  • dangerous
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Nsanoqonig na mgumi, mutt ta'mpas'g tltaqamoqwa'siw na sipu.
    Translation: That ice is dangerous, don't cross that river carelessly.
