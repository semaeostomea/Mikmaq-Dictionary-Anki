mesiapoqte'g

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she stirs it with an instrument
Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
Meanings:
  • stir (with an instrument)
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ta'n tujiw eltoq petaqan mesiapoqte'g samuqwan aq wape'jgl.
    Translation: Whenever he/she makes a pie he/she mixes water and flour.
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: me·si·a·bohk·teeg

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • mesiapoqte'm -- I stir it -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
  • mesiapoqte'meg -- We stir it -- (first person plural exclusive animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
