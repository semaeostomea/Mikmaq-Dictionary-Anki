elui'tmasit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she takes an oath
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • take oath
  • swear
  • make pledge
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Elui'tmasit, ma nugu' naqana'mgw.
    Translation: He/she has taken an oath, he/she will not drink again.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: e路lu路ii路d锚路ma路sit

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • elui'tmasi -- I take an oath -- (first person singular animate)
  • elui'tmasieg -- We take an oath -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • elui'tmasultieg -- We take an oath -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
