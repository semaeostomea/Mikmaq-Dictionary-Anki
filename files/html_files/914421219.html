gmenie'wit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she receives holy communion
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • receive holy communion
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Welo'teget wen gisgmenie'wij.
    Translation: You feel good after receiveing Holy Communion.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: gê·me·ni·ee·wit

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • gmenie'wi -- I receive holy communion -- (first person singular animate)
  • gmenie'wieg -- we receive holy communion -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • gmenie'wultieg -- we receive holy communion -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
Borrowed Word: from French, communier
Document References:
  • Pacifique Dictionary Manuscript, Page Image #422
    Transcription:gomnieoti et gem. communion
    Expanded:gomnieoti et gemnieoti communion
    Transliteration:gumnie'wit and gmnie’wit, communion
