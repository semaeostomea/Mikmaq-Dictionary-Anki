pelteg

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: It is thick
Part of Speech: verb inanimate intransitive
Meanings:
  • thick (of liquid, porridge)
  • pour thickly
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Mu ge'itu tepian ta'n telpelteg, gatu ta'n tujiw na guta'tu'g nmitesnu.
    Translation: I don't know if it is thick enough, but when we pour it we will be able to tell.
