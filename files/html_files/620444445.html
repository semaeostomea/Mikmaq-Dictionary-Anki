saputasgiet

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she penetrates through
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • penetrate through
  • poke out of
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Suel nugu' saputasgiet na lasqo'plaw.
    Translation: At this point that board is almost sticking all the way through.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: sa·bu·das·ki·et

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • saputasgiei -- I penetrate through -- (first person singular animate)
  • saputasgieieg -- We penatrate through -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • saputasgia'tieg -- We penatrate through -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
