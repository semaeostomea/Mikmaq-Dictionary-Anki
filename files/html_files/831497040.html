temilqunta'tl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she breaks its wings
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • breaks its wings
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ala sisip altejimatl unnesqe'l, etug goqwei gisna wen temilqunta'tl?
    Translation: That waterfowl is dragging its wing, I wonder what or who broke its wing?
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: de路mil路hgun路daa路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • temilqunte'g -- I breaks his/her wings -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • temilqunto'l -- I break your wings -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • temilqunte'lit -- temilqunte'it -- he/she breaks my wings -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
