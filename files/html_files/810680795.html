sapa'latl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she gets him/her through
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • save (from mishap)
  • get through
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Nti wetng nutepaqan aq sapa'lit metuguna'q.
    Translation: My dog, by pulling the sled, got me through the storm.
