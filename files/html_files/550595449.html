lnueiei

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw

  • 1.

    Translation: Of native
    Part of Speech: noun inanimate
    Meanings:
    • pertaining to natives
    • belongs to first nations
    • of aboriginal-origin
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Lnueiei'l gtapegiaqann ni'n maw(i) uls'tmann.
      Translation: I really like the sound of Native songs.
      Recording: Recording by dmm
    Pronunciation Guide: 锚ll路nu路ey路ey

    Related Entry: nnueiei
    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • lnueie'l -- pertaining to natives -- (plural)

  • 2.

    Translation: Of native
    Part of Speech: noun animate
    Meanings:
    • pertaining to natives
    • belongs to first nations
    • of aboriginal
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Nnueie'g tam weja'les'pnig?
      Translation: Where did you get the Native ones?
      Recording: Recording by dmm
    Pronunciation Guide: 锚ll路nu路ey路ey

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • lnueie'g -- pertaining to natives -- (plural)
