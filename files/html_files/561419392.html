siptaqan'g

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is stretching it
Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
Meanings:
  • stretching
  • holding a steady tension
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Amujpa siptaqanm ula qasawo'qapi glaman ma' nisianug.
    Translation: I have to hold a steady tension on this wire to keep it from falling.
