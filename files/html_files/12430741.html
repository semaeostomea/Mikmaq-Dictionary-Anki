elapaqto'sit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she splashes self
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • splash oneself
  • spray oneself
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Mu gesispa'tug goqwei, pas'g elapaqto'sit.
    Translation: He/she didn't wash anything, he/she only splashed his/herself.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: e·la·bahk·too·sit

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • elapaqto'si -- I splash myself -- (first person singular animate)
  • elapaqto'sieg -- We splash ourselves -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • elapaqto'sultieg -- We splash ourselves -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
