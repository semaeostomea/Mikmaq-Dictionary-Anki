ta'puowa'j

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw

  • 1.

    Translation: Second one
    Part of Speech: noun animate
    Meanings:
    • second one
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Na negm ta'puowa'j na'te'l wejimilesit.
      Translation: He/she is the second one to get rich there.
      Recording: Recording by jnw
    Pronunciation Guide: daa路bu路o路waach

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • ta'puowa'jg -- second ones -- (plural)
    • ta'puowa'jl -- a second one -- (fourth person)

  • 2.

    Translation: Duece
    Part of Speech: noun animate
    Meanings:
    • deuce(playing cards)
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Ta'puowa'jg nesungig, gatu gi'l?
      Translation: I have three dueces, how about you?.
      Recording: Recording by dmm
    Pronunciation Guide: daa路bu路o路waach
