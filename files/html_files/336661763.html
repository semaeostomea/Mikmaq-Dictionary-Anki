wettepne'g

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she tries to catch up with it
Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
Meanings:
  • try to catch up with
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Gina'sit, gwitnn wettepne'gl.
    Translation: He/she ties hard, trying to keep up with the canoes.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: wet·te·bê·neeg

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • wettepne'm -- I try to catch up with it -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
  • wettepne'meg -- We try to catch up with it -- (first person plural exclusive animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
