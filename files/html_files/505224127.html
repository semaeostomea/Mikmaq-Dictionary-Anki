a's'guatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she meets someone
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • come across
  • meet
  • encounter
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Najia's'guatl ta'n tujiw oqwa'nij.
    Translation: He/she goes to meet him/her when he/she arrives.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: aa路s锚路gu路a路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • a's'gaq -- I meet someone -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • a's'gul -- I meet you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • a's'guit -- He/she meets me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
