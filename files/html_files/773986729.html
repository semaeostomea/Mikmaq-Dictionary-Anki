sepusepilatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she closes him/her/it by tying with rope or string
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • close by tying with rope or string
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Go'qoli'gwejl sepusepilatl muntiigtug.
    Translation: He/she tied the chicken inside the bag.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: se·bu·se·bi·la·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • sepusepilg -- I close him/her by tying with rope or string -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
