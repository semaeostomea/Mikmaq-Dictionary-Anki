peji'taqamat

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she trips
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • trip (over)
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ge'sg alnenaqa'si suel peji'taqamai.
    Translation: As I was rushing about, I almost tripped.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: be路jii路da路hga路mat

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • peji'taqamai -- I trip over -- (first person singular animate)
  • peji'taqamaieg -- We trip over -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • peji'taqama'tieg -- We trip over -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
