etawaqtmat

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she begs
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • beg
  • ask for favor
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Etawaqtmat ula's'tn ms't goqwei.
    Translation: He/she implores that all goes well.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: e路da路wahk路t锚路mat

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • etawaqtmai -- I beg -- (first person singular animate)
  • etawaqtmaieg -- We beg -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • etawaqtma'tieg -- We beg -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
