amalo'qomanimusi

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Fruit bush
Part of Speech: noun animate
Meanings:
  • fruit bush
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Mawpugwelgig tet na amalo'qomanimusi'g.
    Translation: There are lots of fruit bushes here.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: a·mal·oo·hko·man·i·mu·si

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • amalo'qomanimusi'g -- fruit bushes -- (plural)
  • amalo'qomanimusi'l -- a fruit bush -- (fourth person)
Document References:
  • Pacifique Dictionary Manuscript, Page Image #6
    Transcription:amalŏgŏmanel, manimosi, arb fruits "
    Expanded:(amalŏgŏmanel), amalŏgŏmanimosi, arbre/arbuste aux fruits
    Transliteration:(amalo’qomane’l), amalo'qomanimosi fruit bush
