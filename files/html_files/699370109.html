igatne'suinu

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Racer
Part of Speech: noun animate
Meanings:
  • racer
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ta'sijig igatne'suinug esgmaljig?
    Translation: How many racers are you waiting for?
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: i·ga·dê·nee·su·i·nu

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • igatne'suinu'g -- horse racers -- (plural)
  • igatne'suinu'l -- a horse racer -- (fourth person)
