najitugwa'latl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is going to wake him/her up
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • going to wake up someone
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Mali najitugwa'latl ugwe'ji'jl.
    Translation: Mary is going to wake her sister up.
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: na·ji·du·gwaa·la·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • najitugwa'lg -- I am going to wake him/her up -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • najitugwa'lul -- I am going to wake you up -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • najitugwa'lit -- He/she is coming GISNA going to wake me up -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
