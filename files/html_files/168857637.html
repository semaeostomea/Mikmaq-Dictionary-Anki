eli'sewatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she sews for him/her
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • sew for
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Wo'guma eli'sewaji.
    Translation: He/she sews for his/her family members.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: e路lii路se路wa路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • eli'sewg -- I sew for him/her -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • eli'sewul -- I sew for you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • eli'sewit -- He/she sews for me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
