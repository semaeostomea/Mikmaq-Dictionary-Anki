apu'jenatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she warms him by body heat
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • warm up by using body
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Apu'jenatl unjann.
    Translation: He/she keeps his/her child warm (with his/her body).
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: a路buu路je路na路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • apu'jeng -- apu'jenk -- I warm him up by my body -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • apu'jenul -- apu'jenul -- I warm you up by my body -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • apu'jenit -- apu'jenit -- He/she warms me up by his/her body -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
