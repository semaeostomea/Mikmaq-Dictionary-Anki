niganpuguit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw

  • 1.

    Translation: Leader
    Part of Speech: noun animate
    Meanings:
    • leader
    • stand at the front
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Wen niganpuguit ta'n etellugwen?
      Translation: Who is the boss where you work?
      Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
    Pronunciation Guide: ni·gan·bu·gu·it

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • niganpugultijig -- nikan'pukultijik -- leaders -- (plural)
    • niganpuguilitl -- nikan'pukuilitl -- a leader -- (fourth person)

  • 2.

    Translation: He/she stands ahead
    Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
    Meanings:
    • stand ahead
    • leader
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Pie'l na ula tet niganpuguit aq negm tlimultew ta'n tllugwetes.
      Translation: Pierre is the leader here and he will tell you what you will be doing.
      Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
    Pronunciation Guide: ni·gan·bu·gu·it

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • niganpugui -- nikan'pukui -- I stand ahead -- (first person singular animate)
    • niganpuguieg -- nikan'pukuiek -- We stand ahead -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
    • niganpugultieg -- nikan'pukultiek -- We stand ahead -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
