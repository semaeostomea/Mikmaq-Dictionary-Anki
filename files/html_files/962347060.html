samtesguatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she grazes someone
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • graze
  • brush against
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Tlia' pas'g gijga' samtesgaj, guietew.
    Translation: Even if you just brushes against him/her/it slightly, he/she/it will topple.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: sam路tes路ku路a路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • samtesgaq -- I graze him/her -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • samtesgul -- I graze you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • samtesguit -- He/she grazes me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
