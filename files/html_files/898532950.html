asgaja't

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she moves with a limp
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • walk with a limp
  • limp
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Nti asgaja't, mu gejiaq ta'n wejiet.
    Translation: My dog is limping, I don't know where he came from.
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: as路ka路jaad

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • asgaja'i -- I move with a limp -- (first person singular animate)
  • asgaja'ieg -- we move with a limp -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • asgajo'ltieg -- we move with a limp -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
