gaqapija'latl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she hangs someone
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • hang up
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Gaqapija'latl ugnepamgeweiml.
    Translation: He/she hangs up his/her nightgown.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: ga·hga·bi·jaa·la·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • gaqapija'l'g -- I hang him up -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
