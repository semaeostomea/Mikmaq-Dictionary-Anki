pemapaqto'sit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is going along splashing self
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • splash self (as going along)
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Lpa'tu'ji'j pemiet aq pemapaqto'sit sitmug.
    Translation: The little boy is going along at the beach and splashing himself.
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: bema·bahk·too·sit

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • pemapaqto'si -- I am going along splashing myself -- (first person singular animate)
  • pemapaqto'sieg -- We are going along splashing ourselves -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • pemapaqto'sultieg -- We are going along splashing ourselves -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
