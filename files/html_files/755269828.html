ugs'tqamuit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she comes into the world
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • born
  • come into world
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Mijua'ji'j ugs'tqamuip sepei.
    Translation: A baby was born this morning.
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: uk路s锚t路hka路mu路it

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • ugs'tqamui -- I am born -- (first person singular animate)
  • ugs'tqamuieg -- We are born -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • ugs'tqamultieg -- We are born -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
