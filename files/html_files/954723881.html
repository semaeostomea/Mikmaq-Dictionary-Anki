mawisetesguatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she crumples...with feet
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • crumple
  • crumple with feet
  • kicks it together
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Mawisetesguatl ugtatla'ieml gesgmnaq panta'tug ga'qan.
    Translation: He/she crumples up his/her shirt with his/her feet before opening the door.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: ma·wi·se·des·ku·a·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • mawisetesgaq -- I crumple...with my feet -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
