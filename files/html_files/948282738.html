natanguat

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she goes shopping
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • go shopping
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Negmaq maja'sitaq natanguataq.
    Translation: He/she left to go shopping.
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: na路dan路ku路at

Alternate Grammatical Forms:

  • natanguaieg -- We are going shopping -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • natangua'tieg -- We are going shopping -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
