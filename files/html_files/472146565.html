asamaja'toq

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she does it fancily
Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
Meanings:
  • do fancily
  • do elegantly
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Malsusan asamaja'toq wi'g.
    Translation: Mary-Susan really beautifies her house.
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: a路sa路ma路jaa路dohk

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • asamaja'tu -- I do it fancily -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
  • asamaja'tueg -- We do it fancily -- (first person plural exclusive animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
