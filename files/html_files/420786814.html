matu't'g

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she eats a bit of it
Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
Meanings:
  • eat a bit of
  • eat leftovers of
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Pas'g matu't'g wilu muta mu gewisinug.
    Translation: He/she just picks at his/her food because he/she isn't hungry.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: ma路duu路d锚k

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • matu'tm -- I eat a bit of it -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
  • matu'tmeg -- We eat a bit of it -- (first person plural exclusive animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
