nujine'pateget

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw

  • 1.

    Translation: Executioner
    Part of Speech: noun animate
    Meanings:
    • executioner
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Welta'si mu nugu'lugowaqan eigtnug ugjit nujine'pateget.
      Translation: I'm glad this is no longer work for an executioner.
      Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
    Pronunciation Guide: nu·ji·nee·ba·de·get

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • nujine'pategejig -- executioners -- (dual)
    • nujine'pataqatijig -- executioners -- (plural)
    • nujine'pategelitl -- an executioner -- (fourth person)

  • 2.

    Translation: Serial killer
    Part of Speech: noun animate
    Meanings:
    • serial killer
    • murderer
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Masqwa'lut na, muta nujine'pateget.
      Translation: He/she is going to be put away because he/she is a murderer.
      Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
    Pronunciation Guide: nu·ji·nee·ba·de·get

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • nujine'pategejig -- serial killers -- (dual)
    • nujine'pataqatijig -- serial killers -- (plural)
    • nujine'pategelitl -- a serial killer -- (fourth person)
