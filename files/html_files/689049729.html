jipaluejit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Phobic
Part of Speech: noun animate
Meanings:
  • phobic
  • fearful person
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Na ewle'jit gajuewji'j mawjipaluejit.
    Translation: That unfortunate kitten is afraid of everything..
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: ji·ba·lu·e·jit

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • jipaluejig -- phobics -- (dual)
  • jipalua'tijig -- phobics -- (plural)
  • jipaluejilitl -- a phobic -- (fourth person)
