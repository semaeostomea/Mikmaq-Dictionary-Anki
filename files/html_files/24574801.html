nenaqte'maqan

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Fast song or fast chant
Part of Speech: noun inanimate
Meanings:
  • fast song
  • fast chant
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Nenaqte'maqan wels'tm.
    Translation: I like the fast tune.
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: ne路nahg路dee路ma路hgan

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • nenaqte'maqann -- fast songs, fast chants -- (plural)
