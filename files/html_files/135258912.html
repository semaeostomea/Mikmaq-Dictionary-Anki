wissuignematl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she overpowers him/her/it
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • overpower
  • overcome
  • conquer
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Je'g wissuignematl umisl.
    Translation: Jack overpowers his older sister.
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: wis·su·i·gê·ne·ma·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • wissuignemg -- I overpower someone -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • wissuignemul -- I overpower you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • wissuignemit -- He/she overpowers me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
