npisune'gaqtmat

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she gathers/provides remedies for him/her self.
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • Gathers/provides remedies for him/her self
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Nsugwisaq nata' npisune'gaqtmap, aq elg mimajuinu'g i'neia'tuapni ta'n npisun telmawo'tmg.
    Translation: My late aunt knew how to gather her own medicines, and she also showed others how to gather medicine.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
