pepga'latl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she thins it out or down
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • thin out (liquid)
  • thin down (board)
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Nujimilamugwa'teget amujpa pepga'latl ugpent'ml gesgmnaq e'wagw.
    Translation: The painter must thin his/her paint before he/she uses it.
