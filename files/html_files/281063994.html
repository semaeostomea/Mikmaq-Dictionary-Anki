wejgu'aqamit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is due
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • due
  • have right to
  • owed
  • qualify for compensation
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Nitap wejgu'aqamit pugwelg suliewei.
    Translation: My friend has lots of money coming to him\her.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: wej·kuu·ahk·am·it

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • wejgu'aqami -- I am due -- (first person singular animate)
  • wejgu'aqamieg -- We are due -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • wejgu'aqamultieg -- We are due -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
