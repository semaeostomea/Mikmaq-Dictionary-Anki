nasuoqpugua'sit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she stands astraddle
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • stand astraddle
  • stand astride
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ji'me'j gis nasuoqpugua'sit altesgasimgewei, gisgaje'g igatne'wen.
    Translation: James is standing astride of his bicycle, he is ready to race.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: na·su·ohk·pu·gu·aa·sit

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • nasuoqpugua'si -- I stand astraddle -- (first person singular animate)
  • nasuoqpugua'tieg -- We stand astraddle -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • nasuoqpuguitaieg -- We stand astraddle -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
