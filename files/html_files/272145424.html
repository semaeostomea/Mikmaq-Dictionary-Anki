we'gwata'latl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she frightens someone
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • frighten
  • scare
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Teliawana'gwi'tmat lpa we'gwata'lit te's tepa'lij na utepaqang.
    Translation: He/she is such a bad driver he/she scares me everytime he/she gives me a ride in his/her vehicle.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: wee·gwa·daa·la·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • we'gwata'l'g -- I frighten someone -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • we'gwata'lul -- I frighten you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • we'gwata'lit -- He/she frightens me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
