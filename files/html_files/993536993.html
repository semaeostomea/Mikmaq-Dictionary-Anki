metpit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is not wearing outerwear
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • not wear outerwear
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Tuma ale'g gujmug, metpit.
    Translation: Tom is hanging around outside, without outerwear.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: met路bit

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • metpieg -- we are not wearing outer wear -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • metpultieg -- we are not wearing outer wear -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
Document References:
  • Pacifique Dictionary Manuscript, Page Image #708
    Transcription:etre assis pres dun feu (au dehors), metpai;
    Transliteration:to be sitting near the fire (outdoors/outside), metpai;
    Normalized source as entry:metpit, he/she is not wearing outerwear
    Public Note:On page of "pogteo, fire" related words.
    Public Note:The Speakers Group discussion for metpai lead to concluding that metpit, he/she is not wearing outerwear, should be added and recorded for the dictionary.
  • Dictionary of the language of the Micmac Indians : who reside in Nova Scotia, New Brunswick, Prince Edward Island, Cape Breton and Newfoundland, Page Image #123
