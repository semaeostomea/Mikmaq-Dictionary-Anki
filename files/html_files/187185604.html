plu'jaqamati

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm

Translation: Mirror
Part of Speech: noun inanimate
Meanings:

Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ge' jugwasgnmui na plu'jaqamati, taliulm'tu'n.
    Translation: Would you pass me that mirror, please.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: bê·luu·ja·hga·ma·di

Related Entry: angamsutiei
