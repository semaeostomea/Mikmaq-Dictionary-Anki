naqasueta'tl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she beats out the fire on him/her/it
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • beat out fire
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Gisnaqasueta'tl miti'sl gesgmnaq wasamamglennigw.
    Translation: He/she was able to put the fire out before it burnt the tree too badly.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: na·hga·su·e·daa·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • naqasuete'g -- I beat out the fire on him/her -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • naqasueto'l -- I beat out the fire on you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • naqasuete'lit -- He/she beats out the fire on me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
