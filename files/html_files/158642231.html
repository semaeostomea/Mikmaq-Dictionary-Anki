maqanimatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she verbally outwits him/her
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • verbally outwit
  • verbally embrass
  • verballly baffle
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Maqanimatl ugjignaml, toqotujiw wesgewe'g.
    Translation: He/she verbally outwitted his/her brother, then he/she laughs.
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: ma路hga路ni路ma路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • maqanimg -- I verbally outwit him/her -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • maqanimul -- I verbally outwit you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • maqanimit -- He/she verbally outwits me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
