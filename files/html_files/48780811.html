migultiat

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He experiences the pregnancy symptoms of partner
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • experience (by male) pregnancy symptoms of partner
  • become irritated (as first child) when mother carries her second child
  • sympathy pains (by male)
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ntjignam migultiap amgwesewe'l ugs'tqamuiniteg unjann.
    Translation: My kid brother had sympathy pains while his first child was being born.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: mi·gull·di·at

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • migultiai -- mikul'tiai -- I am experiencing my partner's pregnency symptoms -- (first person singular animate)
  • migultiaieg -- mikul'tiaiek -- We are experiencing our partners' pregenancy symptoms -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • migul'tia'tieg -- mikul'tia'tieg -- We are experiencing our partners' pregenancy symptoms -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
