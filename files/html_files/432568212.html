pemigegwi'pit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is running along slowly
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • run along slowly
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Wejo'tg gsigawa'sin, gatu ejela'toq, pemigegwi'pit.
    Translation: He/she tries to go fast, but can't help it, he/she goes along slowly.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: be路mi路ge路gwii路bit

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • pemigegwi'pi -- I am running along slowly -- (first person singular animate)
  • pemigegwi'pieg -- We are running along slowly -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • pemigegwi'pultieg -- We are running along slowly -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
