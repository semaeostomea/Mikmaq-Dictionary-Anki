elisqanapega'toq

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she casts on
Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
Meanings:
  • cast on in knitting
  • cast off in knitting
  • weave base of basket
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Nige' elisqanapega'tu ula ligpenigen
    Translation: Now I'll weave the base for this basket.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: e路lis路hka路na路be路gaa路dohk

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • elisqanapega'tu -- I cast on -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
  • elisqanapega'tueg -- We cast on -- (first person plural exclusive animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
