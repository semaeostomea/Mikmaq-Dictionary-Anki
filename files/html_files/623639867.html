alsumatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she has control of him/her
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • have control of
  • own
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ugti'l alsumatl.
    Translation: He/she has control of his/her dog.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: al·su·ma·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • alsumg -- I control him/her -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • alsumul -- I control you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • alsumit -- He/she controls me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
