jipaqatesing

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is startled/terrified
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • startled
  • terrified
  • scared
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Jipaqatesingg'p wenji'guomg wetnisieteg.
    Translation: He/she was terrified when he/she fell off the house.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: ji路ba路hga路de路sink

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • jipaqatesineg -- we are startled -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • jipaqatesultieg -- we are startled -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
