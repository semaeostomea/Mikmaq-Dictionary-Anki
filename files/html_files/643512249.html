esnamqa'toq

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she squeezes it together
Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
Meanings:
  • squeeze together
  • press together
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Gisapangitut na smtug esnamqa'toq ugtsulieweim.
    Translation: He/she squeezes his/her money together as soon as he/she gets paid.
