jigg'puteget

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she rakes
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • rake
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Matle'n metuwite'tg jigg'putegen.
    Translation: Madelaine is not looking forward to raking.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: jik路k锚路bu路de路get

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • jigg'putegei -- I rake -- (first person singular animate)
  • jigg'putegeieg -- We rake -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • jigg'putaqatieg -- We rake -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
