gomgwejui'gaqan

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Mi'gmaq hieroglyphics
Part of Speech: noun inanimate
Meanings:
  • glyphics (Nnu/Mi'gmaw)
  • hieroglyphics (Nnu/Mi'gmaw)
  • character used to write Micmac
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Awije'jit esgwiet ta'n gisgitg gomgwejui'gaqan.
    Translation: Very few people are left who can read Mi'gmaq hieroglyphics.
