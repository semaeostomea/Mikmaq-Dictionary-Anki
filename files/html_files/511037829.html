nenaqewistoq

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is speaking rapidly
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • speaking rapidly
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Nenaqewistoq, ma' pitewistug.
    Translation: He/she speaks rapidly, he/she will not talk for long.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: ne路nahg路ew路is路tohk

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • nenaqewistueg -- we speak rapidly -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • nenaqewistu'tieg -- we speak rapidly -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
Document References:
  • Pacifique Dictionary Manuscript, Page Image #580
