gesmigit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is well·built
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • shapely
  • well·built
  • well·proportioned
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Gesmigit na wasuego'q.
    Translation: That vase is shapely.
