sisla'gwa'toq

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she fries it
Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
Meanings:
  • fry
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Wen gis sisla'gwa'toq ula pipnaqan?
    Translation: Who fried this bread?
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: si·sê·laa·gwaa·dohk

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • sisla'gwa'tu -- I fry it -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
  • sisla'gwa'tueg -- We fry it -- (first person plural exclusive animate subject, third person singular inanimate object)
