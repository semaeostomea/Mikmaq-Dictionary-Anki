Translation: It is rapid water
Part of Speech: verb inanimate intransitive
Meanings:
  • rapid water
  • rapid water preceeding a waterfall
Example of word used in a sentence:

    Translation: Once you float to the fast water preceeding a waterfall you are going down the falls.
