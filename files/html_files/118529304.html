metewatalg

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: It(horse) makes a sound when eating
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • makes a sound when eating
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Lpa apigji'j nutaq metewatalg!
    Translation: I can hear a mouse eating!
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: me路de路wa路dalk
