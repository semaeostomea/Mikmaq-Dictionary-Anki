Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm

Translation: One hundred
Part of Speech: noun
Meanings:
  • one hundred
  • hundred (in counting)
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Gasg'ptnnaqan tesagl pisoqos'til.
