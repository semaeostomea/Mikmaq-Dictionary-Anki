getgatalg

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she eats ill-manneredly
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • eat ill·manneredly
  • eat disgusting food
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ta'n wen jagejuatalg mawgetgatalg.
    Translation: Whom ever eats to clean out (by eating) eats ill-manneredly.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: get·ka·dalk

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • getgatal -- I eat ill-manneredly -- (first person singular animate)
  • getgataleg -- we eat ill-manneredly -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • getgatalultieg -- we eat ill-manneredly -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
