pgesign'ji'j

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Small piece of cloth
Part of Speech: noun inanimate
Meanings:
  • small piece of cloth
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Natanguaigw nespnnmnej pgesign'ji'j, gulaman gisnmitesnu ta'n tegenn welwije'tigl.
    Translation: When we go shopping let's take a small sample of the cloth, so we can see what things go well together.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
