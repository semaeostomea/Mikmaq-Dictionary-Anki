gesispa'tegemgewei

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Cloth or other object used for washing
Part of Speech: noun inanimate
Meanings:
  • object pertaining to washing
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ula epit gesispa'tegemgewei.
    Translation: Here is the cloth you use for washing.
    Recording: Recording by ewm
