teppilatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she hoists him/her on board
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • hoist aboard
  • haul or rope on board
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Pugjig na teppilul ala gtul, aq e'wa's ula a'papi.
    Translation: Soon I'm going to hoist you onto your boat and I'll use this rope.
