eppaqsijig

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: They are warm (as in milk)
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • warm (as in milk)
  • luke warm
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ula mlagejg eppaqsijig.
    Translation: This milk is luke warm.
    Recording: Recording by ewm
