getu'muatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she makes music for him.
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • make music for
  • entertain
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Nmijgamij getu'muatl nnjann.
    Translation: My grandfather plays a tune for my child.
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: ge路duu路mu路a路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • getu'maq -- I make music for him/her -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • getu'mul -- I make music for you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • getu'muit -- He/she makes music for me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
