  • 1.

    Translation: Breakfast
    Part of Speech: noun inanimate
    Meanings:
    • breakfast
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Wigapu'g ula egsitpu'gowei gisitu.
      Translation: This breakfast I've made tastes good.
      Recording: Recording by dmm
    Pronunciation Guide: ek路sit路puu路go路wey

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • egsitpu'gowe'l -- breakfasts -- (plural)

  • 2.

    Translation: Pertaining to morning
    Part of Speech: noun inanimate
    Meanings:
    • item pertaining to morning
    • event pertaining to morning
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Egsitpu'gowei wi'gatign jugwa'tui.
      Translation: Bring me the morning paper.
      Recording: Recording by ewm
    Pronunciation Guide: ek路sit路puu路go路wey

    Alternate Grammatical Forms:
    • egsitpu'gowe'l -- mornings' -- (plural)
