pelqa'toq

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm

Translation: He/she peels it
Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
Meanings:
  • peel
  • husk
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: We'gwimesgi'g ugjigi wejipelqa'toq uggwatg.
    Translation: What a large scab he/she just peeled off of his/her foot.
