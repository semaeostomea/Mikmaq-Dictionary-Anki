piamite'lmatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she thinks more of him/her
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • think more of
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Mali me' piamite'lmatl ugtgajue'wjml je mu ugtnmu'jml.
    Translation: Mary thinks more of her cat than her dog.
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: bi路a路mi路deel路ma路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • piamite'lmg -- I think more of him/her -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • piamite'lmul -- I think more of you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • piamite'lmit -- He/she thinks more of me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
