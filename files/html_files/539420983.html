wetmuatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she gives someone a smoke(cigarette)
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • give smoke (cigarette) to
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ge' ugtmui?
    Translation: Can you share a smoke?
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: we·dê·mu·a·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • wetmaq -- I give him/her a smoke(cigarette) -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • wetmul -- I give you a smoke(cigarette) -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • wetmuit -- He/she gives me a smoke(cigarette) -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
