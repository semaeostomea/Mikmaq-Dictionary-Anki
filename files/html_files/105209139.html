mpisune'gati

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Area of medicinal plants
Part of Speech: noun inanimate
Meanings:
  • area of medicinal plants
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Nugmij geitoq ta'n eteg mpisune'gati.
    Translation: My grandmother knows where there is a medicine field.
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: 锚m路pi路sun路ee路ga路di

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • mpisue'gati'l -- medicine fields -- (plural)
Document References:
  • Pacifique Dictionary Manuscript, Page Image #529
    Transcription:mpisongati, mpisonaganitg (A.M), contr茅e, terre aux [herbes] plants m茅d.
    Expanded:mpisongati, mpisonaganitg (Abb茅 Maillard), contr茅e, terre aux [herbes] plants m茅dicines
    Transliteration:mpisune'gati, mpisunaqanitg (Abb茅 Maillard) region, land of [herbs] medicinal plants
  • Dictionary of the language of the Micmac Indians : who reside in Nova Scotia, New Brunswick, Prince Edward Island, Cape Breton and Newfoundland, Page Image #179
