sepp'saqa'latl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she/it sticks it into
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • stick into
  • penetrate into
  • pierce into
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Malpale'wit sangew sepp'saqa'latl saqati'l ngatg.
    Translation: The doctor gently puts the needle into my foot.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: sep路p锚路sa路hgaa路la路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • sepp'saqa'l'g -- I stick it into -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
