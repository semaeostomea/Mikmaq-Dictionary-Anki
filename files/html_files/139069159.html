gepsga'latl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she puts him/her to sleep
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • put to sleep
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Unjann gepsga'latl.
    Translation: He/she puts his/her child to sleep.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: geps路kaa路la路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • gepsga'l'g -- I put him to sleep -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • gepsga'lul -- I put you to sleep -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • gepsga'lit -- He/she puts me to sleep -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
