gimelmugtasit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she laughs at self in a secret manner
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • laugh at oneself in a secret manner
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Sosep pemiet aq pemgimelmugtasit, etug goqwei gisio'pla'toq?
    Translation: Joseph is going by and secretly laughing at himself, I wonder what he has done wrong?
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: gi·mel·muk·ta·sit

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • gimelmugtasi -- I laugh at myself in a secret manner -- (first person singular animate)
  • gimelmugtasieg -- We laugh at ourselves in a secret manner -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • gimelmugtasultieg -- We laugh at ourselves in a secret manner -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
