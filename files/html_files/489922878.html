esp'puguig

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: It stands tall
Part of Speech: verb inanimate intransitive
Meanings:
  • stand tall
  • stand on higher plane
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: UIa lgusuaqan esp'puguig.
    Translation: This ladder is up high.
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: es·pê·bu·gu·ik

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • esp'puguigl -- They stand high up -- (third person plural inanimate)
