pisuilsu'tmuatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she falsely accuses him/her
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • accuse falsely
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Goqwei wejipisuilsu'tmatioq, wijig'tioq!
    Translation: Why are you falsely accusing one another, you're siblings!
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: bi·su·il·suu·dê·mu·a·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • pisuilsu'tmaq -- I falsely accuse him/her -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • pisuilsu'tmul -- I falsely accuse you -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • pisuilsu'tmuit -- He/she falsely accuses me -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
