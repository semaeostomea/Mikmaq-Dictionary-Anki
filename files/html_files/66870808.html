piamigsalatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm

Translation: He/she prefers him/her
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • prefer
  • likes better
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Geitaqal newte'tnitl me' piamigsalatl je mu igtigl.
    Translation: I know that he/she likes one of them more than the other.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
