tma'gittaqan

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm

Translation: Saw
Part of Speech: noun inanimate
Meanings:
  • saw
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: We'gwigaqi'smilamu'gl tma'gittaqann eltas'gl gisgug.
    Translation: Definitely there are many kinds of saws made today.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
