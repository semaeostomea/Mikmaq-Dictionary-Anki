gasgawgtig

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: At the roadside
Part of Speech: noun inanimate
Meanings:
  • at roadside
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Gasgawgtig malipqwanjl etlintuisgetasgl.
    Translation: Hazelnuts are being sold at the roadside.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: kas路kawg路tig

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • gasgawgti'l -- side of the roads -- (plural)
