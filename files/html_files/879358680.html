wejintijig

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw

  • 1.

    Translation: They fight over it
    Part of Speech: verb inanimate intransitive
    Meanings:
    • fight over (something inanimate)
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Wijei wi'gatign wejintijig.
      Translation: They fight over the same book.
      Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
    Pronunciation Guide: we·jin·di·jik

  • 2.

    Translation: They fight over him/her/it
    Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
    Meanings:
    • fight over (something or someone animate)
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Wejintijig tlawo'ql.
      Translation: They fight over the hunting knife.
      Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
    Pronunciation Guide: we·jin·di·jik
