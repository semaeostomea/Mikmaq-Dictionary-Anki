anguoweie'get

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she hunts for fur
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • hunts for fur
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Newtipug na Sa'n anguoweie'get.
    Translation: All winter John hunts fur.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: an·ku·o·wey·ee·ket

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • anguoweie'geieg -- We hunt fur -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • anguoweie'ga'tieg -- We hunt fur -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
Document References:
  • Pacifique Dictionary Manuscript, Page Image #598
    Transcription:angoōoeiegei, gatitjig, faire cette chasser, oōoēgan, [tto] fourrure
    Expanded:angoōoeiegei, angoōoeigatitjig, faire cette chasser, angoōoēgan, [tto] fourrure
    Transliteration:anguoweiegei, anguoweigatijig, do this hunt, anguo'we'gan, [tto] fur
