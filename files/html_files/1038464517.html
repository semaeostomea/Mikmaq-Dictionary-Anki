gepitnetoqsit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Sounds nasal
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • sounds nasal
  • sounds hoarse
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Etug tgeia'tat, tewjigepitnetoqsit.
    Translation: Maybe he/she has a cold, he/she sounds so nasally.
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: ge路bi路d锚路ne路dohk路sit

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • gepitnetoqsi -- I sound nasally -- (first person singular animate)
  • gepitnetoqsieg -- We sound nasally -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • gepitnetoqsultieg -- We sound nasally -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
