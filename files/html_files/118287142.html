welia'su'na'q

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is well·dressed
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • well·dressed
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ji'nm pegising ga'qanigtug aq welia'su'na'q
    Translation: A well dressed man just came to the door.
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: we·li·aa·suu·naahk

Related Entry: welgutat
Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • welia'su'na'm -- I am well·dressed -- (first person singular animate)
  • welia'su'na'meg -- We are well·dressed -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • welia'su'na'mu'tieg -- We are well·dressed -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
