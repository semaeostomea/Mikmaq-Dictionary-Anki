tale'g

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw

  • 1.

    Translation: At what time...?
    Part of Speech: verb inanimate intransitive
    Meanings:
    • at what time?
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Tale'gtetew a's'gati'gw?
      Translation: At what time shall we(dual) meet?
      Recording: Recording by dmm
    Pronunciation Guide: da路leeg

  • 2.

    Translation: What is wrong with him/her?
    Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
    Meanings:
    • what is wrong with...?
    • how is he/she?
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Me' tale'g nitap?
      Translation: How's that friend of mine?
      Recording: Recording by ewm
    Pronunciation Guide: da路leeg
