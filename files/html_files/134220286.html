peigwaqatg

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she stays here and there
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • stay here and there
  • live all over
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Tuma gaqipeigwaqatg ula Listugujg.
    Translation: Thomas has stayed here and there in Listuguj.
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: bey路kwa路hgatk

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • peigwaqatm -- I stay here and there -- (first person singular animate)
  • peigwaqatmeg -- We stay here and there -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • peigwaqatmu'tieg -- We stay here and there -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
