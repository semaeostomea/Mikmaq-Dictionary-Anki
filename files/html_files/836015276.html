toqjuapegit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she crawls up
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • crawl up
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Tewjigispnet lpa toqjuapegit unpo'qoneg.
    Translation: He/she was so tired he/she crawled up into his/her bed.
    Recording: Recording by ewm
Pronunciation Guide: dohk路chu路a路be路git

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • toqjuapegi -- I crawl up -- (first person singular animate)
  • toqjuapegieg -- We crawl up -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • toqjuapegultieg -- We crawl up -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
