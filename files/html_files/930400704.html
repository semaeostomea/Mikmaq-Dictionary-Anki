elu'jing

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is bent (at waist) gaping toward
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • bent(at waist) gaping toward
  • lean toward
  • crane toward
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Nmu'ji'j elu'jing ta'n ni'n eliei.
    Translation: The dog leans toward the direction I am going in.
