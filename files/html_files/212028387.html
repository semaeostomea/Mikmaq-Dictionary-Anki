wasoqota'tl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she polishes it (pot)
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • polish
  • shine
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Tio'tis i'wasoqota'tl ugpesmgeweiml.
    Translation: Eunice polishes her religious metal.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: wa·so·hgo·daa·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • wasoqote'g -- I polish him\her\it -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
