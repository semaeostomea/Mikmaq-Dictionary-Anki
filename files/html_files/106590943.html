paqsipgeitelua'tl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she shot and kills him/her/it instantly
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • shoot and kill instantly (with gun or bow and arrow)
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: A' na nige' wen paqsipgeitelua'tl igtigl?
    Translation: Okay, so who shot and instantly killed the other one?
    Recording: Recording by dmm
Pronunciation Guide: bahk路sip路gey路te路lu路a路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • paqsipgeitelaq -- I shot and kill him/her/it instantly -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • paqsipgeitelul -- I shot and kill you instantly -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • paqsipgeiteluit -- He/she shot and kills me instantly -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
