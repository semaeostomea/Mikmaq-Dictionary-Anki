najitgismit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is going to go swim swim
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • going to go swim
  • set out to swim
  • going to bathe in the river (older meaning)
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Teluet najitgismit gaqa'tegej.
    Translation: He/she says he/she is going swimming when he/she is finished what he/she is doing.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: na·jit·gi·sêm·it

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • najitgismi -- I am going to go swim -- (first person singular animate)
  • najitgismieg -- we are going to go swim -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • najitgismultieg -- najitgismu'tieg -- we are going to go swim -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
