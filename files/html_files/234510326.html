weiteja'latl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she drove someone from there
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • drive from
  • test路drive
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Muliang weiteja'latl ujjl.
    Translation: He/she drove his/her father from Montreal.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: weey路te路jaa路la路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • weiteja'l'g -- I drove someone from there -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • weiteja'lul -- I drove you from there -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • weiteja'lit -- He/she drove me from there -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
