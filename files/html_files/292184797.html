weleiatijig

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: They treat each other well
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • treat each other well
  • kind to each other
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Tewjisa'q tepqatgig gatu me' weleiatijig.
    Translation: They have been married so long but they still treat each other good.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: we·ley·a·di·jik

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • weleiaq -- I treat him/her well -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • weleiul -- I treat you well -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • weleiwit -- He/she treats me well -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
Document References:
  • Pacifique Dictionary Manuscript, Page Image #234
    Transcription:5. oeleiatigo, attitude mutuelle (bonne)
    Transliteration:5. weleiati’gw, mutual attitude (good)
