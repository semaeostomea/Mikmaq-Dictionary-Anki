pangwenewet

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He /she delouses
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • delouse
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Malpale'wi'sgw na jijuaqa pangwenewet.
    Translation: The nurse sometimes delouses.
    Recording: Recording by ewm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: ban·kwe·na·wet

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • pangwenewei -- I delouse -- (first person singular animate)
  • pangweneweieg -- We delouse -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • pangwenewa'utieg -- We delouse -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
