poqjigtapegiet

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she begins to sing
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • begin to sing
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Pujig poqjigtapegia'tijig, pas'g pe'l esgmala'titl ula gaqewo'gwenin.
    Translation: They will start singing soon, but first they are waiting for this person to finish speaking.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: bohk·chik·ta·be·gi·et

Related Entry: poqjintoq
Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • poqjigtapegiei -- I begin to sing -- (first person singular animate)
  • poqjigtapegieieg -- We begin to sing -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • poqjigtapegia'tieg -- We begin to sing -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
