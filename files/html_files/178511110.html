esgutesmatl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she keeps one unbroken
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • he/she broke all but one
  • keep one unbroken
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Etug nugu' pas'g newte'tnitl esgutesmatl.
    Translation: There must be only one left that he/she hasn't broken.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: es·ku·de·sê·ma·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • esgutesmg -- I keep one unbroken -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
