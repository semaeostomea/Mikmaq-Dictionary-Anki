guntewe'gati

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: Rocky land/quarries
Part of Speech: noun inanimate
Meanings:
  • rocky land
  • rock quarry
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Gutewe'gatigtug wejiei, mawte'man guntal ugjit pugto'guom.
    Translation: I've been to the rock quarry, I'm selecting rocks for a fire place.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: gun路dew路ee路ka路di

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • gutewe'gati'l -- rocky lands/rock quarries -- (plural)
