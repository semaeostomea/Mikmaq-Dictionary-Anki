pegitewo'gwet

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she talks a long time
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • talk long time
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ugjo'tes gisgug mu ugsamipgitewo'gwen.
    Translation: Today I will try not to speak for too long.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: be·gi·de·woo·gwet

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • pegitewo'gwei -- I talk a long time -- (first person singular animate)
  • pegitewo'gweieg -- We talk a long time -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • pegitewo'g'tieg -- We talk a long time -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
