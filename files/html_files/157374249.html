niwa'latl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she dries it out
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • dry out
  • empty all liquid out
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Lpa'tu'ji'j gina'sit malige'wl niwa'latl, etlwe'watl suppinn.
    Translation: The little boy is working hard to empty out the barrel, using a pint dipper.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
