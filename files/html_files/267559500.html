sesupe'g

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw

  • 1.

    Translation: He/she/it is slippery
    Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
    Meanings:
    • slippery
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Sesupe'g ula plamu muta tewjisgmoqonit.
      Translation: This salmon is slippery because he/she has this slimy coating on him/her.
      Recording: Recording by ewm
    Pronunciation Guide: se路su路beeg

  • 2.

    Translation: It is slippery
    Part of Speech: verb inanimate intransitive
    Meanings:
    • slippery (due to weather)
    Example of word used in a sentence:
    • Text: Mgumi'gnig awti, etna' sesupe'g.
      Translation: The road is icy, it must be slippery.
      Recording: Recording by jnw
    Pronunciation Guide: se路su路beeg
