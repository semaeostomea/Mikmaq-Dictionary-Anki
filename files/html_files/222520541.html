elisgnuatg

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she braids it
Part of Speech: verb inanimate transitive
Meanings:
  • braid
  • knit
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Mali elisgnuatg mesgi'g a'su'n.
    Translation: Mary knits a large blanket.
