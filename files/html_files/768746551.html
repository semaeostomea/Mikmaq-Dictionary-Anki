wajua'latl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she fills it (bottle) up with non·liquid
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • fill up (with non·liquid)
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: La'taqsunn wajua'latl aq poqtapilatl.
    Translation: He/she fills the pail (with non-liquid) and carries it away.
    Recording: Recording by dmm 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: wa·ju·aa·la·dêl

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • wajua'l'g -- I fill it up with non·liquid -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
