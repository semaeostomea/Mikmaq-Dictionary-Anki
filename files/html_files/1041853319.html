usgitqamug

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm

Translation: Surface of the earth
Part of Speech: noun inanimate
Meanings:
  • surface (of the earth)
  • above ground
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Gi's nige' asugom te'sisga'q je ugumujin te'sipunqeg wigi ula usgitqamug.
    Translation: It's been sixty eight years that I have been living on the surface of this earth.
