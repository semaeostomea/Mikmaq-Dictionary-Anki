natawitpluteget

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she is a good law maker
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • good law maker
  • good decision maker
  • good promiser
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Natawitpluteget gatu awisiw tela'teget ta'n telitplumsij.
    Translation: He/she makes many promises, but rarely keeps any.
    Recording: Recording by jnw 🔉
Pronunciation Guide: na·da·wit·pê·lu·de·get

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • natawitplutegei -- I am a good law maker -- (first person singular animate)
  • natawitplutegeieg -- We are good law makers -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • natawitplutaqatieg -- We are good law makers -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
