ginateja'sit

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she struts on
Part of Speech: verb animate intransitive
Meanings:
  • strut on
  • proudly going
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Ginateja'tieg ta'n tuji'w gisgespu'tueie'g.
    Translation: We were strutting along after we defeated everyone.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: gi路na路de路jaa路sit

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • ginateja'si -- I strut on -- (first person singular animate)
  • ginateja'tieg -- We strut on -- (first person dual exclusive animate)
  • ginatejitaieg, ginateja'sultieg -- We strut on -- (first person plural exclusive animate)
