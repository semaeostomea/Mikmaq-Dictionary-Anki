elipqamu'latl

Recordings:
  • Recording by dmm
  • Recording by ewm
  • Recording by jnw
Translation: He/she slides him/her toward
Part of Speech: verb animate transitive
Meanings:
  • slide toward
  • roll toward
Example of word used in a sentence:
  • Text: Uggwe'ji'jl qame'g elipqamu'latl.
    Translation: He/she slides his/her little sister across to the other side.
    Recording: Recording by jnw
Pronunciation Guide: e路lip路hka路muu路la路d锚l

Alternate Grammatical Forms:
  • elipqamu'l'g -- I slide him toward -- (first person singular animate subject, third person singular animate object)
  • elipqamu'lul -- I slide you toward -- (first person singular animate subject, second person singular animate object)
  • elipqamu'lit -- He/she slides me toward -- (third person singular animate subject, first person singular animate object)
